## 내용

Who can use this feature

Available on the [Professional, Organization, and Enterprise plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273)

[Team admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439) only on the Professional plan

[Organization admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439) only on the Organization or Enterprise plans

When a user is removed from your plan, any drafts they created in the team or organization are collected into a new project folder in **Admin**. To prevent important work from getting lost, admins can access and manage these drafts.

**More details:**

- On the Organization and Enterprise plans, any guests removed from your organization will also get their drafts moved to **Admin**
- If you upgrade from the Starter plan to the Professional plan, drafts from any previously removed users will be available in **Admin**
- If someone shares a draft before they are removed, the people invited to the file can keep viewing and editing it

## Manage unassigned drafts

![](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/28287138256919)

Admins can move individual drafts into a project—or their entire drafts project into a team—so other people can pick up where the original draft creator left off. You can also permanently delete the removed users’ project or files.

### Move the unassigned project to a team

Moving the project into a team automatically gives everyone in the team access to it. Whether people can view or edit the project’s files depends on their team-level permissions.

Professional Organization and Enterprise

Moving an unassigned project to your team is not currently supported on the Professional plan.

1. From the file browser, click  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select the **Content** tab.
3. Click the **Unassigned drafts** tab.
4. Navigate to the project you’d like to move and click .
5. Click **Move**.
6. Choose a team in the organization to move the project into.

### Move a file in the drafts project to a different project

Professional Organization and Enterprise

1. From the file browser, click  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select the **Content** tab.
3. Click the **Unassigned drafts** tab.
4. Navigate to the project you’d like to review and click its name.
5. Find the file you’d like to move and click .
6. Click **Move**.
7. Choose a project to move the file into.

### Delete a drafts project

Deleting a project from **Unassigned drafts** permanently removes it—and all its files—from your team or organization. There is no way to restore a project deleted from **Admin**.

Professional Organization and Enterprise

1. From the file browser, click  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select the **Content** tab.
3. Click the **Unassigned drafts** tab.
4. Navigate to the project you’d like to delete and click .
5. Click **Permanently delete**.
