Before you Start

Who can use this feature

 

Users on [any plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273-Choose-a-Figma-Plan)

Change your preferred language for the Figma website, apps, and marketing emails. You can also use Google’s Chrome browser and its machine translation to [translate Figma into more languages →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4404356228119-Change-Figma-s-language-using-Google-Chrome)

Figma's browser, [desktop app](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/5601429983767), and [mobile app](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500007537281) are currently supported in English, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish (Spain and Latin America), and Portuguese (Brazil).

# Change your language preference in Figma

## From a file or the file browser

To change your preferred language from within a Figma file or the Figma file browser:

1. Click  in the bottom-right corner.
2. Click **Change language**.
3. Choose your preferred language.
4. Click **Save**.

Figma will refresh the current tab and display the chosen language. You must manually refresh any other open tabs to see the change.

## From your account settings

To change your preferred language from [your Figma account settings](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500006061462-View-and-manage-account-settings):

1. From the Figma file browser, click your avatar in the top-left corner.
2. Select **Settings**.
3. Under **Language**, click **Change language.**
4. Choose your preferred language and click **Save**.
5. Figma refreshes the current tab and displays the chosen language. You must manually refresh any other open tabs to see the change.

## On a mobile device

You can change the language displayed in the Figma mobile app by updating the preferred language in your mobile device settings.

1. Use one of the following to change the language on your mobile device:

   - Android devices: [Change the language on your Android device](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/32047?hl=en&co=GENIE.Platform%3DAndroid)
   - iOS devices:
     - [Change the language on your iPhone or iPad](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204031)
     - You can also change the language preference for just the Figma app by navigating to **Settings** > **Figma** and selecting a language from the **Preferred Language**dropdown menu.
2. Restart the Figma mobile app.
