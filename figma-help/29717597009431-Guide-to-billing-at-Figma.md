## 내용

In March 2025, Figma introduced updates to how seats and other billing features work.

[Learn more about the transition to the updated billing model](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/27468498501527).

## How billing works at Figma

Your Figma subscription cost is determined by two key factors: your plan type and the number of Full, Dev, and Collab seats in your plan. You have access to these seats for the duration of your billing term.

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Plan** | Your Figma plan determines the available feature set for each product. | - Starter - Professional - Organization - Enterprise |
| **Seats** | Seat types determine which products your users have access to. You purchase a single seat per person, and the seat type determines their product access. | - **Full**: Figma Design, Dev Mode, FigJam, Figma Slides, Figma Make, Figma Draw - **Dev**: Dev Mode, FigJam, Figma Slides - **Collab**: FigJam, Figma Slides - **View** (free seat): View and comment access to all files |

To see the cost of each seat type on each plan, check out [Figma’s pricing page](https://figma.com/pricing).

**Tip**: Have specific needs for your plan? Explore these additional offerings:

- [**Figma for Government**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/22932461365015): Built for government agencies, with enhanced security and compliance controls.
- [**Governance+**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31825370509591) (Enterprise add-on): Advanced admin and security features for greater control and visibility.
- [**Figma for Education**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061214): Free access for eligible students and educators, designed for classroom use.

## Select a plan

First, you’ll need to create an account and select which plan fits your needs.

- [Create a Figma account](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039811114): Get started with Figma by creating an account.
- [Figma plans and features](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273): See an overview of the features available on each of Figma’s plans.

## Manage people and their seats

Once you’ve chosen a plan, you’ll want to consider which products your individual users need access to and how you'll manage new users joining your Figma team or organization.

- [Manage seats in Figma](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039960434): Learn about the different seat types, how assign a seat type to each individual user, and how to change a user’s seat type.
- [Manage seats via SCIM](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360048787534): Organizations using SAML SSO can use SCIM to manage user seats in Figma.
- [Request a seat upgrade](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040453433): Learn how users can request seat upgrades from admins.

![Manage user seats from the People tab of your admin.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/30168374689943)

![The seat approval settings modal. Set seat approval settings by seat type.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/30287974536727)

You'll also decide how to manage incoming seat requests.

- [Set approval settings for new seats](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4414038570007): Choose if new users can automatically get a new seat when they join, or if you’ll require manual approvals. By default, you can automatically approve requests only if you have pre-purchased seats available.
- [Approve or decline seat requests](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500003870721): Review how to approve or decline new seat requests that require manual approval.
- [Seat upgrade notifications](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/15506350302615): Get notified when user seats are upgraded via automatic approval.

## Manage your plan, billing, and invoice details

Learn more about how billing works on your plan and how to manage payment details.

- [Manage billing on the Professional plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034): Understand the specifics of billing for your Professional plan, including options for annual vs. monthly billing, seat adjustments at renewal time, and adding new seats during the billing period.
- [Manage billing on the Organization and Enterprise plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328293): Understand the specifics of billing for your Organization or Enterprise plan, including adjusting seats at renewals, adding seats during a billing period, and quarterly invoicing.
- [Manage AI credits](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/35865276858647): Review options for purchasing additional AI credits for your plan.
- [Upgrade or downgrade your plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360046216313): Review how to change your plan type.
- [View and manage payment and invoice details](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040532093): Review invoice details, manage billing contacts, add VAT ID, change payment methods, update company name and address, and more.
- [Pay for Figma](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/10278790616215): Learn how to pay for your Figma subscription.
- [Supported billing currencies](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/13496718847255): Review the currencies you can use when paying for your Figma plan.
- [Sales tax and VAT](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4418838088983): Figma calculates tax based on your billing and shipping addresses.
