## 내용

The Enterprise plan is built for large organizations with complex team structures, compliance needs, and advanced security requirements.

The Enterprise plan might be right for you if you:

- Are a business designing for multiple products or brands
- Need enterprise-level security
- Want scalable design systems and simpler admin management

## Products and features

Enterprise customers have access to the full Figma product suite:

- [Figma Design](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/categories/360002042553)
- [FigJam](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/categories/360002051633)
- [Figma Slides](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24170630629911-Explore-Figma-Slides)
- [Figma Draw](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31440394517143-Explore-Figma-Draw)
- [Figma Sites](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31230436657815-Explore-Figma-Sites)
- [Figma Buzz](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31271566667543-Guide-to-Figma-Buzz)

Enterprise-specific capabilities include:

- [SAML SSO and SCIM provisioning](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040532333-Guide-to-SAML-SSO)
- [Workspaces for managing teams, people, and resources](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/7576392133527)

To better understand which individual features are available on the Enterprise plan, you can:

### Enterprise plan billing

The Enterprise plan uses an annual subscription with quarterly invoices. Your subscription cost is based on the number of Full, Dev, and Collab seats. View seats are free.

Note: Billing is separate from permissions. A paid seat gives someone access to Figma products, while permissions determine what they can do in specific files or projects.

To learn more:

- [Manage billing on the Enterprise plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328293)

### Enterprise plan permissions

In Figma organizations, you can share resources at multiple levels: organization, team, project, file, and prototype.

[Learn more about sharing and permissions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500007609322-Guide-to-sharing-and-permissions).

The following specifications apply to the Enterprise plan:

- You can invite [members or guests](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557314967-Members-versus-guests). Guest refers to anyone in the organization that doesn't have a company email, and may have limited access to the organization.
- Set organization access on files, projects, or teams at the workspace level. Learn more about organization [access to teams](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039970673-Team-permissions#h_01K6NBT5RPHEE8JD14YY8JD0YS), [projects, or files](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/35361119554711-File-and-project-permissions).
- [Viewer restrictions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040045574-Restrict-copying-and-sharing-on-files): Prevent viewers from copying, sharing, or exporting file content.
- [Password protection](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/5726720100247-Add-password-protection-to-files-and-prototypes): Add a password to restrict access to shared files.
- Organization admins can [disable public link sharing for the organization](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/5726756336791-Manage-public-link-sharing-and-open-sessions). This prevents people outside the organization from accessing files via link sharing.
- From the [advanced share settings](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500007609322), you can toggle whether organization members can discover files via search in the file browser. If discovery is disabled, members need a direct link or invitation to the file, team, or project.
- Draft files are always private until you share them. You can invite collaborators to your draft files with either `can view` or `can edit` access, depending on your [seat type](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039960434).
  - For example, if you have a Collab seat, you can invite others to your FigJam and Figma Slides drafts, but not to Figma Design drafts, since a Collab seat does not include Figma Design access.
