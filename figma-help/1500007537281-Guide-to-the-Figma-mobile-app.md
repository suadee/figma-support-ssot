## 내용

Before you start

Who can use this feature

 

Available on [any team or plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273-Plans-and-teams-in-Figma).

Anyone with at least `can view` access can view and leave comments on files, boards, slide decks, and prototypes

Anyone with `can edit` access can mirror frames from their desktop

The Figma mobile app lets you access your files and test prototypes from anywhere, including on your mobile device or iPad. You can also share files, browse multiple workspaces, and mirror frames from your desktop to your mobile device.

![Three Figma mobile app screens showing recent files, comment activity, and frame mirroring options.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/28418850518039)

Use the Figma app on your mobile device or iPad:

- Search, view, and comment on files, boards, slide decks, and prototypes
- Present and test prototypes without being tethered to your desktop
- Mirror selected frames from your desktop to a mobile device
- Navigate teams and projects
- Mark files and projects as favorites for fast access
- Stay in the loop when working outside of Figma with push notifications

Note: It's not possible to edit Figma Design files or Figma Slides decks using the mobile app.

## Download the mobile app

Use the following links or QR codes to install the Figma mobile app:

- [Figma for iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/figma-and-figjam/id1152747299): Supported on devices running iOS 16 or later
- [Figma for Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.figma.mirror&hl=en_US&gl=US): Supported on devices running Android 8 or later

For best performance, we recommend that you use the latest version of your device’s operating system.

[![Apple App Store download button with adjacent QR code for Figma mobile app installation.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/28418882080535)](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/figma-and-figjam/id1152747299)

[![Google Play Store download button with adjacent QR code for easy installation of the Figma mobile app.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/28418850522135)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.figma.mirror&hl=en_US&gl=US)

Note: The Figma mobile app isn’t supported on beta versions of iOS or Android operating systems.

## Log in to Figma

Log in to your Figma account using any of these methods:

- [Email address and password](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041064554#email)
- [Google Single Sign On](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041064554#google)
- [Third-party identity provider (SAML SSO)](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041064554#SAMLSSO)

Having trouble logging in on your iPad? Make sure you enable cookies for Safari. Go to **Settings** > **Safari** > **Privacy & Security** and disable **Block all cookies**. If that doesn't work, try logging in to your account in the Safari browser first, then open the Figma app.

## Add and switch to a different organization or account

You can sign in and switch between multiple Figma accounts in the mobile app. To do so:

1. Tap the icon in the top right corner to visit **Settings**.
2. Tap **[Your name >]**.
3. Select another logged in account, or click  to add a new account and log in.

## Set preferred language

Figma's browser, [desktop app](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/5601429983767), and mobile app are currently supported in English, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish (Spain and Latin America), and Portuguese (Brazil). You can change the language displayed in the Figma mobile app by updating the preferred language in your mobile device settings. [Change your language preference →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/6956360971415-Change-your-language-preference)

## Browse and search files

When you open the Figma mobile app, you'll see your recent **Prototypes** and **Files** organized in their own section.

![Figma mobile app showing recent files with thumbnails and labels under the Recents tab.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/28418850524055)

There are a couple ways to find a file or prototype:

- Browse the **Recents** tab to view the files and prototypes you most recently opened
- Click **See all** for a section to browse all your files or prototypes
- Use the **Search** tab to find a specific file for prototype by name. You can also browse through the files you starred using the Figma desktop or browser apps

Note: You cannot star or unstar files from the Figma mobile app.

Tap on a file or prototype to open and view it.

## View a Figma Design file

With a Figma Design file open, you can:

- Hold and drag your finger across your screen to pan around the file's canvas
- Pinch the screen with two fingers to zoom in and out of the canvas
- Long-press a location on the canvas to add a comment

Tap the  menu to access these additional options:

- Show or hide comment indicators on the canvas
- View prototypes
- Manage which users have access to the file
- View the comments list to see all comments in the file
- Switch between pages in the file

![Figma mobile app showing menu options: hide comments, view prototype, manage users, and open pages.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/28418850525463)

## Preview Figma Make files on mobile

Figma Make is an AI-driven, prompt-to-app tool that lets you bring ideas and existing Figma designs to life as functional prototypes, web apps, and interactive UI.

Use Make in the Figma iOS or Android mobile app to:

- Preview a functional prototype, web app or interactive UI with Figma Make
- Share Make files
- View and reply to comments

Note: The ability to create, view, and edit Makes using the Figma Mobile app is not available at the moment.

To get started:

1. From the app, select an existing Make file from **Recents**.
2. From here, you can interact with the preview of the app or press  to view and reply to **Comments**.

[Learn more about using Figma Make](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304485164695-Create-and-edit-a-functional-prototype-or-web-app).

## Edit FigJam boards on iPad

You can view and edit FigJam boards on the go within the Figma iPad app. Use FigJam to:

- **Draw:** Sketch, take notes, highlight, and annotate with precision using the Apple Pencil
- **Brainstorm:** Collect and organize photos, links, quotes, and ideas
- **Diagram:** Build out flows and wireframes with shapes and connectors
- **Decorate:** Add flair to boards with shapes, washi tape, stickers, and stamps
- **Try something new:** Explore hundreds of templates for planning and personal projects in the Figma Community

## View a prototype

View a prototype to test interactions or to view static designs on a real device. If a prototype has no interactions, tap on the screen to navigate to the next frame.

![Mobile app viewing of prototype of a wallet balance and recent transactions with timestamps and icons.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/28418850527383)

Prototypes have a hidden menu bar. To access it, press and hold the screen with two fingers. The menu contains options to:

- Switch between [flow starting points](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039823894-Set-prototype-device-and-starting-point#Starting_point)
- Restart the prototype from the [flow's starting frame](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039823894)
- View the file for the prototype
- Turn comments on and off
- Toggle [hotspot hints](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040318013-Present-designs-and-prototypes#Show_Hotspot_Hints)
- Share the [prototype link](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039822654)
- Exit the prototype and return to the **Recent** tab

![](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/28418850529559)

[Learn how to view prototypes on a mobile device →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040321093)

## View Figma Slides decks

With a Figma Slides deck open, you can:

- Navigate to each slide in the deck by tapping the slide preview at the bottom of the screen
- Pinch the screen with two fingers to zoom in and out on your currently selected slide
- Long-press a location on a slide to add a comment
- Rotate your device to landscape and see the slide deck in full screen

Tap the  menu to access these additional options:

- Show or hide comment indicators on the deck
- Manage which users have access to the deck
- View the comments list to see all comments on the deck

![Mobile view of a Figma Slides deck example, with slide thumbnails at the bottom and a slide titled "Syllabus" displayed.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/28418850532247)

## Comment on files and prototypes

Use comments on the Figma mobile app to keep up-to-date even when you’re away from your computer. You can access your comment notifications from the **Activity** tab.

Tap on a comment to open the quick reply view. This lets you quickly view comments without having to load the entire file. From here, you can:

- Reply to comments
- Long-press on a comment to leave a reaction
- Tap  to resolve a comment you added

If you want to view the comment within the larger context of the file, tap **Open comment in file**.

![Mobile interface displaying a Figma file with comments and quick reply feature open.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/28418882095127)

## Mirror frames

Use the **Mirror** tab of the mobile app to view frames that are selected in the file on your desktop.

- View real-time edits to a frame on a mobile device
- Preview how assets in your design look scaled to a device screen

[Learn how to mirror frames on a mobile device →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040321093)

## Manage push notifications

Stay up-to-date with mobile push notifications, even when you’re working outside of Figma. Push notifications are enabled by default.

To manage push notifications in the Figma mobile app:

1. Tap your organization’s logo in the top-right corner.
2. Tap **Notifications**.
3. Use the settings to configure your push notification preferences

## Troubleshoot app closure on Android devices

If you’re experiencing an issue with the Figma mobile app forcibly closing on your Android device, do the following:

1. Update the [Android System Webview](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.google.android.webview&hl=en_US&gl=US&pli=1) on your device to the latest version.
2. Restart your device.
3. Open the Figma mobile app.

## Troubleshoot access issues on iOS devices with Lockdown Mode enabled

If you’re using an iOS device with [Lockdown Mode](https://support.apple.com/en-ca/105120) enabled, you may receive an error when accessing Figma using a browser. To resolve this issue, you must exclude figma.com from Lockdown Mode on your device. For instructions on excluding websites from Lockdown Mode, see [Apple’s help documentation](https://support.apple.com/105120).
