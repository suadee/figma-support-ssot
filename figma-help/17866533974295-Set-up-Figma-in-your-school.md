## 내용

Figma is the leading web-based, collaborative design tool that can allow your Education organization to create, share, and ideate together in real time.

Qualified high school organizations are granted access to Figma’s Enterprise license plan.

[Learn more about Figma for Education](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061214-Figma-for-Education).

As a Figma administrator, you will be able to manage user permissions, workspaces, teams, and implement controls for your organization’s access and privacy.

Below are the basic steps and articles needed for setting up Figma for Education in the Google Workspace as well as Figma admin panel for your school or district:

1. [Create your Figma account and verify education status](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061214-Figma-for-Education)
2. [Set up Figma with Chrome App licensing](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/17866896241687)
3. [Test Figma organization access](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/17867675040663)
4. [Configure your Figma organization](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/17867860467863)
5. [Start using Figma in class](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/17868229061271)
