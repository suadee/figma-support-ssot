## 내용

Before you start

Who can use this feature

The Starter and Professional plans include a single team.

On the Organization and Enterprise plans, any member can create a new team, unless an admin has [restricted team creation](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/10386065399319).

Teams are designated spaces for projects and files. Use teams to collaborate with others, or to organize your work.

[Learn more about teams](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039480614-Manage-a-Starter-or-Professional-team).

You can create or join multiple teams from a single Figma account. The way you create and join teams depends on your plan type:

- Starter plan: The Starter plan is free and includes a single team. You can create one Starter team per account.
- Professional plan: The Professional plan includes a single team. You can have multiple teams on your account. Figma manages the subscription for each team separately.
- Organization and Enterprise plans: The Organization and Enterprise plans each include unlimited teams. Within an organization, you can create as many teams as you need. You can also join multiple teams within an organization, making it possible to work cross-functionally and share resources.

**Are you a student or educator?** You may be eligible for a free Education team.

[Learn more about Figma for Education](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061214).

## Create a team

If you are new to Figma and looking to create a team, create a Figma account. As a part of account creation, you will be prompted to create your team name.

If you already have a team and want to create another one, you can:

- Create a new Professional team on a new subscription
- Create a team within an existing organization, available to those already on an Organization or Enterprise plan. This does not create a new subscription.

### Create a new Professional team

If you are new to Figma and looking to create a team, create a Figma account. As a part of account creation, you will be prompted to create your team name.

If you already have an existing account and want to create a new Starter or Professional team plan:

1. Open the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183-Guide-to-the-file-browser) by logging in to your account at [figma.com](https://figma.com/).
   - If you’re in a specific Figma file, click the  Figma menu > **Back to files.**
2. Click the  dropdown next to your current team name.
3. Select  **Create new**.
4. Give your team a name and click **Create team**.
5. Figma will give you the option to invite collaborators to your team. Enter your team member's email addresses, separated by a comma, to invite them to your team. If you want to invite collaborators later, click **Skip for now**.
6. Select a plan for your team. If you select Professional, you’ll be prompted to [select seat types](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039960434-Manage-seats-in-Figma) for each team member and enter payment information.

Note: New Professional teams are independent of any of your existing plans, and by clicking **Create Team** you agree to [Figma's Terms of Service](https://www.figma.com/legal/tos/).

![Select your team name dropdown menu from the left sidebar.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/35463302733463)

**Tip**: You can also create a team by visiting this link: <https://www.figma.com/files/create-team>

### Create a new team within an organization

On the Organization and Enterprise plans, you can create a new team within your existing organization.

Unless an [admin has restricted team creation](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/10386065399319), any member of an organization can create a team. Guests aren't able to create teams in the organization.

To create a new team in an organization:

1. Open the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183-Guide-to-the-file-browser) by logging in to your account at [figma.com](https://figma.com/).
   - If you’re in a specific Figma file, click the  Figma menu > **Back to files.**
2. Navigate to your teams:
   1. On the Organization plan or Enterprise plan without workspaces enabled, select  **All teams** from the left sidebar.
   2. On the Enterprise plan with workspaces enabled, select  **All workspaces** from the left sidebar. Navigate to the workspace where you'd like to create a team and click into it.
3. Click the  **Team** button in the top-right corner.
4. Enter a name for the team, [set the organization access to the team](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039970673), and click **Next**.
5. [Optional] Enter the names of people you’d like to invite to the team and set their [team permissions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039970673).
6. Click **Create team.**

![Create a new team in an organization.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/35463257625495)

**Tip**: Organization admins can also create new teams from **Admin.**

1. Open **Admin** and click the  **Content** tab.
2. At the top of the team list, click **Create new team**.

## Frequently asked questions

**How can I view members of a team?**

To view members of a team:

1. Open the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183-Guide-to-the-file-browser) by logging in to your account at [figma.com](https://figma.com/).
   - If you’re in a specific Figma file, you can access the file browser by clicking the  Figma menu > **Back to files.**
2. Open a team page.
   - On the Organization or Enterprise plan without workspaces enabled, select  **All teams** from the left sidebar. Then, select a team.
   - On the Enterprise plan with workspaces enabled, select  **All workspaces** from the left sidebar. Then, select a team from the **Your teams** tab.
3. Click  **Open team dropdown** next to the team name and select **View members**.

**What are team permissions?**

Team permissions determine which team-level actions a user can take, such as creating projects within that team, adding or removing team members, and managing team settings.

On the Organization and Enterprise plans, team permissions also allow you to determine if and how users in your organization can access your team.

[Learn more about team permissions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039970673).
