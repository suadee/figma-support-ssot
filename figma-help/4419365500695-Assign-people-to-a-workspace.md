## 내용

Who can use this feature

Available on the [Enterprise plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273)

Organization admins and workspace admins only

[Workspaces](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4409676189207) are collections of teams, people, and resources within a Figma organization.

Assigning people to a workspace lets organization admins delegate user and content management responsibilities to [workspace admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439). Assigning people to a workspace won’t affect their membership to any teams they’ve already joined. While people can only get assigned to one workspace, they’ll still be able to browse, join, or ask to join teams in other workspaces in your organization.

Anyone who isn't assigned to a [workspace](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4409676189207) is considered **Unassigned**. Both organization and workspace admins can manage unassigned users.

- Organization admins can assign people to–or move people between–any workspace within the organization.
- Workspace admins can only assign people to–or move people between–workspaces they manage.

## Assign any unassigned people to a workspace

Organization admins Workspace admins

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select the  **People** tab. Figma will show all members by default.
3. Click the **Workspace: All** filter and select **Unassigned**.
4. Navigate to, or search for people you want to assign.
5. In the **Workspace** column of the table, click **Unassigned** and select a new workspace.

**Tip**: To manage multiple people at once, select them in the table and click **Change workspace** from the bulk actions menu at the bottom of the page.

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Make sure the correct workspace is displayed at the top of the tab bar. If necessary, click the name of the organization or current workspace and switch to the correct workspace.
3. Select the  **People** tab and click  **Add members**.
4. Navigate to, or search for, people you'd like to assign and mark the checkbox next to their name.
5. Click **Add members.**

## Reassign people to another workspace

You can reassign people to a different workspace at any time.

- Organization admins can move people between all workspaces.
- Workspace admins can only move people between workspaces they manage.

Organization admin Workspace admin

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select  **People**.
3. Use the  search field to find the member you want to update.
4. In the **Workspace** column, click their current workspace to open the selector.
5. Select another workspace and click **Change workspace** to confirm.

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select the relevant workspace in the sidebar.
3. Select **Members** to view members and guests in that workspace.
4. In the **Workspace** column, click their current workspace to open the selector.
5. Select another workspace and click **Change workspace** to confirm.

## Unassign members and guests

Organization admins and workspace admins can unassign a member or guest from a workspace at any time.

This moves them to **Unassigned**, where they can be assigned to another workspace. This doesn’t affect their membership on any teams.

Organization admin Workspace admin

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select **Members** in the menu bar. Figma will show all members by default.
3. Find the person you want to unassign. In the **Workspace** column, click their current workspace.
4. Select **Unassign** to remove them from their current workspace.

2. Select the relevant workspace in the sidebar.
