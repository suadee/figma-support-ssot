## 내용

Before you Start

Who can use this feature

 

Users who have been approved to sell resources on Community

You cannot unpublish paid resources from Community but you can delist them. Delisted resources are not visible on Community feeds or creator profiles but are accessible by users who have purchased the resource.

Note: If you delist a plugin with a subscription, current subscribers will retain access through the end of the billing cycle.

Delisted resources retain their usage metrics and publishing information including their title, description, and pricing information. To republish a paid resource, follow the same workflow you used to initially publish the resource.

- [Publish files to the Community →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040035974-Publish-Community-files)
- [Publish plugins to the Community →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360042293394-Publish-plugins-to-the-Figma-Community)
- [Publish widgets to the Community →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4410337103639-Publish-widgets-to-the-Figma-Community)

## Delist paid files

1. Open the resource’s page on the Figma Community.
2. Click the **Delist** button at the bottom of the page.
3. Click **Delist** in the dialog to confirm.

## Delist paid plugins or widgets

1. Open a Figma Design or FigJam file on the Figma desktop app.
2. Click the Figma logo in the upper-left corner, and then do one of the following:  
   - Go to a **Plugins**> **Manage plugins**
   - Go to **Widgets**> **Manage widgets**
3. Select **Development** from the dropdown menu.
4. Click  next to the plugin or widget you want to delist and select **Delist.**

## Scenarios that delist a paid resource

Paid resources can also be automatically delisted under certain circumstances. The following table describes each scenario that delists a paid resource and any available actions you may take:

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Scenario** | **What you can do** |
| Your file includes [restricted content](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040035974-Publish-files-to-the-Community#Content_restrictions_in_published_files). | Remove the restricted content and resubmit the file for approval using the [resource review form.](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdFkE9nFZkMks2NIL3VkYHhquOSjA4_Y9bZgd2xe0estkePMQ/viewform) |
| Your Stripe account was disabled. | Resolve the issue with your Stripe account and the resource will be automatically relisted. If you need assistance, contact [Stripe Support.](https://support.stripe.com/) |
| Your Figma account was blocked due to a Terms of Service violation. | If you believe your account was blocked incorrectly, contact [Figma Support](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041057214-Get-help-with-Figma#Contact_support) to appeal the decision. Resources will be relisted if your account is unblocked. |
| Your Figma account was deleted. | The resource will be permanently delisted. |
| Your resources was removed due to a [DMCA takedown](https://www.dmca.com/FAQ/What-is-a-DMCA-Takedown). | The resource will be permanently unavailable for all users, including those who purchased it. |
