## 내용

**What:** Use the Figma desktop app to download your debug logs.

**Why:** When you contact Figma support, you may be asked to send over a copy of your debug logs to assist us with troubleshooting your issue. These logs provide extra context to help us recreate the issue.

**Using Figma in the browser?**  Learn how to view and capture errors from the developer console in our [Export console logs](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/19818634038935) article.

## Download debug logs on macOS

1. With the desktop app open, click **Help** in the menu bar.
2. Hover over **Troubleshooting**, and click **Save Debug Info**.
3. Choose a location to download the debug log.
4. Click **Save**.

![Menu selection in Figma on macOS for saving debug information via Help > Troubleshooting > Save Debug Info.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/19589190748823)

## Download debug logs on Windows

1. With the desktop app open, click **Help** in the taskbar.
2. Hover over **Troubleshooting**, and click **Save Debug Info**.
3. Choose a location to download the debug log.
4. Click **Save**.

![Accessing "Save Debug Info" under Troubleshooting in Figma's Help menu on Windows.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/19589206852887)
