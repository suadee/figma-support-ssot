## 내용

The Professional, Organization, and Enterprise plans require a paid Figma subscription. Admins can view their next invoice date from the **Billing** page of the file browser.

[Learn more about billing at Figma](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/29717597009431).

## Update payment information

You can update your payment information from the **Settings** page of your admin dashboard.

[Learn more about updating your payment information](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040532093).

## Failed payments

Every now and then a subscription payment may fail. If a payment fails, Figma will continue to attempt the payment for up to 21 days after the initial failure.

To pay an outstanding invoice, you can do one of the following:

- [Update your payment information](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040532093-View-and-manage-payment-and-invoice-details#change-payment-method). Once you’ve updated your payment method, Figma will automatically attempt payments on all outstanding invoices.
- Pay the Stripe invoice directly.
  - To pay a Stripe invoice, [view your invoice details](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040532093-View-and-manage-payment-and-invoice-details#view-invoice-details) in Figma, and click the invoice number to open the Stripe invoice.
  - If you use a new payment method to pay for the invoice on Stripe, Figma will automatically set this payment method as your default payment method for future charges.

If your payment continues to fail, you may become locked out of your account. During this time, you’ll only be able to view your files and projects, and will not be able to edit them. Once you've made a successful payment, Figma will restore access to your account.
