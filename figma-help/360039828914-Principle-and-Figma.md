## 내용

Principle is an interaction design tool that allows you to bring your digital designs and prototypes to life.

You have a whole host of interactions available; from scrolling or swiping, managing multiple states, building custom animations and super slick transitions. [Learn more about Principle here](http://principleformac.com/).

Once you connect Figma to your Principle account, you can easily import your designs from Figma and start building advanced animations.

With Figma and Principle, your designs look and feel like the real deal.

In this article we'll cover how to:

1. [Connect Figma and Principle](#h_63a495e1-7956-44c2-9857-e9d606e50e66)
2. [Import a Design from Figma](#h_86d778f5-2dff-4216-9f0f-9588af4d7af3)
3. [Disconnect your Figma account from Principle](#h_189c0dc5-430f-4368-8299-7548b8e0aeb9)

# Connect Figma and Principle

You'll need to have an account created with Figma and Principle to get the two connected. *Principle is currently available on macOS only.*

When you first go to import a design from Figma, you will be prompted to enter your Figma account details to authorize the connection. This will only happen the first time you use the *Import from Figma* option.

1. Open up Principle.
2. Head to **File** in the main menu and select **Import from Figma**:![File menu in Principle with "Import From Figma" option highlighted, part of the design import process.](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf06642c7d3a4db66082ca/file-qevjscVvS3.png)
3. Or, click the **Import** button:![Toolbar in Principle showing the Import button, allowing Figma designs to be integrated into the workspace.](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf060e042863215a46b87f/file-7TWLvECquT.png)
4. Select **Figma** from the options:![Dialog box in Principle to connect with Figma, showing options to select "Figma".](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf06362c7d3a4db66082c6/file-8LM9hU57l8.png)
5. Click the **Connect to Figma** button:![Connect to Figma button highlighted in Principle's import dialog, ready for integration setup.](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf06442c7d3a4db66082c7/file-YfUTgK0cYH.png)
6. This will open up a browser window. If you're not logged in, you'll be prompted to login to your Figma account:![Login window with email and password fields, option to sign in with Google, and sign-up links.](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf06832c7d3a4db66082cc/file-DN2WVM93Xu.png)
7. Click **Allow access** to confirm:![Authorization prompt for granting Principle access to Figma account, with "Allow Access" button.](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf066a2c7d3a4db66082cb/file-1FLaD86bcg.png)
8. A dialog box will prompt you to return to Principle:![Permission screen for Principle to access Figma account, with options to allow or deny access.](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf06a3042863215a46b885/file-kYWwWrA3y7.png) 

   Note: If you don't get redirected to Principle directly, you can close the authorization window and return to Principle. While it may not look like it, the connection has now been successfully established.

# Import a Design from Figma

Now that the two applications are connected, you'll be able to import your designs directly into Principle. *Note: It's not possible to export a File to Principle from within Figma*.

You can import all of the Frames from a page in a File, or a selection of Frames.

1. Open up Principle.
2. Head to **File** in the main menu and select **Import from Figma**:![Menu options in Principle showing the file menu with "Import From Figma" highlighted.](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf06642c7d3a4db66082ca/file-qevjscVvS3.png)
3. Or, click the **Import** button:![Import button highlighted in Principle's toolbar for transferring designs from Figma.](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf060e042863215a46b87f/file-7TWLvECquT.png)
4. Select **Figma** from the options:![Dialog box in Principle offering Figma connection option with Connect to Figma button highlighted.](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf06362c7d3a4db66082c6/file-8LM9hU57l8.png)
5. You will then be able to select a **Document** from the field provided. This will show you any *Recently Edited Files.* 

   Note: Any files that have been using Multiplayer (for example, files where multiple editors are collaborating) will be prioritized in the **Recently Edited Files** suggestions.

   ![Dropdown menu showing a list of recently edited Figma documents with "Sample File" selected for import options.](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf06d2042863215a46b887/file-J05fKzFTNk.png)
6. Click **Import Page** to import all Frames on the Page.
7. Or, click **Import Selected Frames** to import any Frames that are *currently selected* in Figma.  
     

   Note: At this point, you can also hop over to Figma and select any specific Frames you want to import. This will update the selection in Principle.
8. A status message will indicate that the layers are currently being rendered by Figma:![Rendering progress bar in Principle showing "Figma is Rendering Layers" for importing designs.](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf06e8042863215a46b88c/file-jmUVCotz3C.png)
9. The Frames will then be added to your Artboard in Principle:![Digital prototypes for an art museum app displayed in Principle, showcasing interactive UI design elements and transitions.](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf06f1042863215a46b88d/file-M9JRV8roRX.png)
10. Each object will be rendered as an individual layer, just like it is in Figma. You can select individual objects in either the *Layers* panel on the left, or directly in the canvas:![Layer panel in Principle showing hierarchy for a login page, including status bar, app info, login fields, and more.](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf06f82c7d3a4db66082d1/file-h1rDWQbV8u.png)

# Disconnect your Figma account

If you want to change the Figma account associated with your Principle account, or disconnect the two completely, you can do this in Principle.

1. Click on the **Principle** menu in the Apple menu bar.
2. Select **Disconnect from Figma** from the options:![Options menu in Principle showing "Disconnect From Figma" highlighted for account disconnection.](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bcf07992c7d3a4db66082dc/file-BfzYgMHD4B.png)
3. You can then start the Import process again, to reconnect or connect to a different account.

### Working with Shadows in Figma and Principle

*Drop* and *Inner Shadows* will behave differently between Figma and Principle. Figma renders shadows based on the object's position on the canvas, whereas Principle will show the shadow based on the object itself.

This means that rotating an object in Principle will also rotate the location of the object's shadow (versus remaining consistent as it would in Figma).
