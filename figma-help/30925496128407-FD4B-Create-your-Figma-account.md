## Create your Figma account

To start designing, you’ll need a Figma account. If you don’t have an account yet, visit [Figma.com/signup](http://figma.com/signup) to create one. You can sign up using your email address, or with a Google account.

Figma offers both free and paid account options. If you’re just starting out, the free Starter account is a great choice. If you're using Figma for work, you may already have access to a paid plan, which includes access to additional features. Learn more about [Figma’s plans and features](https://www.figma.com/pricing/).

**Tip:** If you're a student or educator, you may qualify for a free version of Figma's Professional plan. Learn how to [verify your education status](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061214-Verify-education-status).
