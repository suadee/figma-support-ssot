## 내용

Who can use this feature

Supported on [any team or plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273).

You need to be logged into more than one Figma account to switch between accounts. [Log in to your Figma accounts →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041064554)

If you're logged in to more than one Figma account, you can quickly switch between them in the file browser.

## Switch to a different account

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click your name at the top-left of the screen.
2. Select the account you want to switch to. If you can't see the account you want to use, click **Add account** to log in to the account first.
3. Figma will open the file browser in that account.

![Account switcher menu in Figma with options to switch accounts, update settings, and manage community profile.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/15145021379607)

**Tip:** You can also view and manage your community profile, installed plugins, and account settings from the account menu. [**Manage your account →**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500006061462)
