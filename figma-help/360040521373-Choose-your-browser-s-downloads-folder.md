## 내용

Before you start

Who can use this feature

Supported on [any team or plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273-Choose-a-Figma-Plan)

You will need to have access to the administration settings of the browser or device you're using.

Figma files will usually be downloaded directly to your **Downloads** folder, or equivalent. Each browser will allow you to check or update your downloads location. We've put together some instructions on how to do this, by browser.

To find out more about configuring your browser for Figma: [Configure your browser for Figma →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039828614)

Google Chrome Firefox Safari Edge

1. Open Chrome and go to the [Chrome Settings page](chrome://settings/).
   - **Mac**: Go to **Chrome > Preferences** in the menu bar or use the `⌘Command` `,` shortcut.
   - **Windows**: Go to **File > Preferences** or click the vertical ellipses in the top right corner and choose **Settings**.
2. Scroll down and click on the **Advanced** link to expand the settings:![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bae5dee042863158cc6e069/file-xKNimr8qmi.png)
3. In the **Downloads** section, you can see what location any downloads will be saved to: ![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bae5df32c7d3a04dd5b1891/file-K8lSaHPYDJ.png)
4. Use the **Change** button to update this to another location (if desired).

1. Open Firefox and go to **Preferences > General** in the main menu:![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bae7007042863158cc6e0db/file-MTfKkeVty2.png)
2. Scroll down to the **Files and Applications** section.
3. In the **Downloads** settings, you can check the location where to **Save files to**:*![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bae70b2042863158cc6e0e4/file-Qu25qb20NV.png)*
4. Click **Choose** to select another folder.

1. Open Figma in Safari.
2. Head to **Safari > Preferences** in the main menu.
3. In the **General** tab, check/update the **File download location**: ![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5aa962fe2c7d3a2c4983093d/images/5bb20b822c7d3a04dd5b24a2/file-RHGuv8RMNB.png)

1. Open Figma in Microsoft Edge.
2. Select **More** **> Settings** and select **View advanced settings**.
3. In the **Downloads** section, select **Change** to update the *Save downloaded files to* location.
