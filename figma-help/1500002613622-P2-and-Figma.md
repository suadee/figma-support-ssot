## 내용

[P2](https://wordpress.com/p2/) is a communication tool for teams, based on private [WordPress.com](https://wordpress.com/) blogs. It provides a platform for calmed, asynchronous discussion — a space to share, discuss, review, and collaborate with your team in one spot, without interruptions.

[Figma live embeds](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039827134) let you:

- Add live Figma files or prototypes to a P2 post or page.
- Showcase your designs with teammates, clients, partners, and others who may not use Figma.
- Collect design feedback from collaborators who can use P2 to voice opinions, use likes, thumbs up, or votes.

# Embed a file or prototype in P2

## In Figma

1. Open the file you want to embed in P2.
2. To link to a specific frame in the Figma file, select the frame you'd like to embed. To embed a prototype, launch the prototype in Figma.
3. Click the **Share** button in the toolbar.
4. Update your [Link Sharing permissions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040531773-Share-Files-with-anyone-using-Link-Sharing#Update_Link_Sharing) to determine who can interact with the embed (Optional).
5. Click **Copy link**.

## In P2

There are two ways to add a Figma live embed to a P2 post, page, or comment:

- Click the  icon in the editor toolbar to open blocks and search for Figma. You can also type `/figma` directly into the editor. Once the block is added, paste the Figma link into the URL field.  
  ![Figma block in P2 editor with field to paste Figma frame, page, or prototype URL and an "Embed" button.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/1500002386002)
- Paste the Figma link into the editor to automatically convert the link into a Figma block.

# Remove a Figma embed

To remove a Figma embed from a P2 post, page, or comment:

1. Enter Edit mode.
2. Select the Figma block you’d like to remove.
3. Delete it.
