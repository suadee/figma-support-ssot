## 내용

The Professional plan is Figma’s entry-level paid plan. It might be a good fit for you if you:

- Are a professional or part of a small team
- Need unlimited files and projects for a single team
- Want advanced prototyping tools and easier dev handoff

Want to compare the Professional plan to Figma’s other plans? [Learn more about Figma’s plans and pricing](https://www.figma.com/pricing/).

- [Figma Design](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/categories/360002042553)
- [FigJam](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/categories/360002051633)
- [Figma Slides](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24170630629911-Explore-Figma-Slides)
- [Figma Draw](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31440394517143-Explore-Figma-Draw)

## Professional plan structure

The Professional plan includes a single team. Professional teams can create unlimited files and projects. Each team can have an unlimited number of members and collaborators.

![A graphic depicting the hierarchy of a Professional plan. Files sit in projects, which sit in a team.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/35463302911383)

With access to team and project-level settings, teams on the Professional plan can better organize and control their design work across products.

### Professional plan billing

Team owners and admins can manage billing on the Professional plan.

Your subscription cost depends on your billing term (monthly or annual) and the number of paid seats—Full, Dev, or Collab. View seats are free.

Note: Billing is separate from permissions. A paid seat gives someone the ability to use certain Figma products, while permissions control what they can do within specific files or projects.

Learn more:

- [Manage billing on the Professional plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034)
- [Guide to billing at Figma](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/29717597009431)
- [Manage seats in Figma](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039960434)

### Professional plan permissions

On the Professional plan, you can share resources like files, prototypes, projects, and teams. You’ll assign `can view` or `can edit`permissions depending on how you want others to interact with each resource.

Teams on the Professional plan also have access to the following advanced sharing features:

- [Project-level access controls](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/35361119554711-File-and-project-permissions): Set project access to `can view`, `can access` (default), or remove team access entirely.
- [Prototype-only sharing](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040531773-Share-files-and-prototypes): Share just a prototype without giving access to the full file.
- [Viewer restrictions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040045574-Restrict-copying-and-sharing-on-files): Prevent viewers from copying, sharing, or exporting file content.
- [Password protection](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/5726720100247-Add-password-protection-to-files-and-prototypes): Add a password to restrict access to shared files.
- You can invite collaborators to your draft files with either `can view` or `can edit` access, depending on your [seat type](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039960434).
  - For example, if you have a Collab seat, you can invite others to your FigJam and Figma Slides drafts, but not to Figma Design drafts, since a Collab seat does not include Figma Design access.

[Learn more about sharing and permissions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500007609322-Guide-to-sharing-and-permissions).
