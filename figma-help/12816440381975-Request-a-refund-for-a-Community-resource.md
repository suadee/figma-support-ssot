## 내용

**Important:** This article is for refunding Community resources purchased using Figma's payment platform. If you purchased a resource from a third-party site, you’ll need to contact the creator about their refund policy. Learn more about [purchasing Community resources](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/11786473291159).

For information on upgrading or downgrading your **Figma plan**, see [this article](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360046216313-Upgrade-or-downgrade-your-plan) instead.

You can request an automatic refund within 24 hours of purchasing a resource that was sold with a one-time payment.

Plugin subscriptions are not refundable but you can cancel the subscription to prevent it from being automatically renewed. When you cancel a subscription, you can still access the plugin until the end of the current billing cycle. Learn more about [managing plugin subscriptions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/11786473291159#Manage_your_plugin_subscriptions).

## Request a refund within 24 hours

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click your avatar in the top-left corner.
2. Select **Settings** from the account menu.
3. Select the **Community** tab.
4. Under Community purchases, locate the resource you want to refund.
5. From the options dropdown, select **Request refund**.![Dropdown menu in Community purchases panel showing options: See order details, Contact creator, Request refund.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/12816612435991)

5. Select a reason for requesting the refund.
6. Click **Confirm**.

Refunds take five to ten business days to appear on your account.

Note: You can also access the **Request refund** option on the resource’s Community page.

## Request a refund after 24 hours

You may request a refund after the 24 hour window has closed. However, requests made after 24 hours are subject to Figma's discretion.

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click your avatar in the top-left corner.
2. Select **Settings** from the account menu.
3. Select the **Community** tab.
4. Under Community purchases, locate the resource you want to refund.
5. From the options dropdown, select **Get support**.
6. Under request type, select **Request a refund**.
7. Fill out the remaining fields.
8. Click **Send**.

If your refund request is approved, you’ll receive the refunded amount in five to ten business days. If your request was denied, you will be notified by email.
