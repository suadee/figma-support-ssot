## 내용

Individuals have rights when it comes to controlling how their data is used.  These Data Subject Rights include things like the right to erase your personal data, the right to have your data updated and corrected, and a right to know what data is being processed.  Figma explains more about your rights in our [privacy policy](https://www.figma.com/legal/privacy/) and we have embedded self service tools to empower you to exercise your rights.

This page explains how to access or delete your personal data through your account, and how to contact us if you need additional help.

## Accessing or deleting your personal data

You can manage your personal data directly within your Figma account:

- View and update your information within [Figma:](https://www.figma.com)
  - [Log in](https://www.figma.com/login) to your Figma account to see your profile and account settings.
- View and update your information within the [Figma Forum:](https://forum.figma.com/)
  - Log in to your Figma forum account via your Figma account to see your profile and account settings.
- Delete your account and associated data:
  - Follow the steps in [this article](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039826814) to permanently remove your account and related data.

## If you are not an account admin or owner

If you are not the account administrator or owner, some self-service tools may not be available to you. For example, you will not be able to delete the account.  In these cases, please contact the account owner or administrator for assistance exercising your rights.

## Additional help with data subject rights

If you are the account admin or owner and cannot exercise your Data Subject Rights using the available self-service tools, our privacy team can assist:

Email **privacy@figma.com** with the subject line: **“Data Subject Rights Request”** and include the following details:

- The **email address** associated with your Figma account
- A brief description of the assistance you need
- Any additional context that may be helpful
