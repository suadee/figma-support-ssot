## 내용

Who can use this feature

Available on
[Professional, Organization, and Enterprise plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273)

Only **organization admins** can manage billing
for an organization

Only **team admins** can manage billing for
a team on the Professional plan

From your **Billing** tab in admin, you can view your billing overview and invoice details. You can also use billing settings to manage details related to your invoicing—such as adding your Tax ID, changing your payment method, updating company name and address, and managing billing contacts.

**Tip**: Have other questions on billing? Check out our [Guide to billing at Figma](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/29717597009431).

## View billing overview

Admins can find their plan summary, subscription details, and invoice history in the **Billing** tab.

1. Open the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183-Guide-to-the-file-browser) by logging in to your account at [figma.com](https://figma.com/).
   - If you’re in a specific Figma file, you can access the file browser by clicking  the Figma menu > **Back to files** from the left sidebar toolbar.
2. Click  **Admin**.
3. Select the  **Billing** tab.

Professional plan
Organization and Enterprise plans

![The billing overview page on the Professional plan.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/38962247421591)

From the **Billing** tab, you can:

- Select the **Invoices** tab to [view your invoice history](#view-invoice-details).
- Review the date your next invoice will be issued, as well as the projected total cost and number of seats included.
- View or [upgrade your current plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360046216313).
- View the total number of seats currently on your team.

## View invoice details

View individual invoice details to see details such as payment method, payment status, and total seats billed for.

1. Open the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183-Guide-to-the-file-browser) by logging in to your account at [figma.com](https://figma.com/).
   - If you’re in a specific Figma file, you can access the file browser by clicking  the Figma menu > **Back to files** from the left sidebar toolbar.
2. Click  **Admin**.
3. Select the  **Billing** tab.
4. Select the **Invoices** tab.
5. Select an invoice to open an invoice details panel.

Professional plan
Organization and Enterprise plans

![Side by side of monthly and annual invoices for the professional plan.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/38962230858391)

- The billing cadence and dates included in the invoice:
  - A monthly invoice. Monthly invoices are sent when you are on a monthly plan, or if you are on an annual plan and add extra paid seats or an AI credits subscription throughout your annual billing term.
  - An annual renewal invoice, which is sent once a year to those on an annual subscription.
- Invoice details, which include:
  - Due date
  - Invoice status
  - Invoice number, which you can click to view the invoice in Stripe
  - Payment method
- Seat charges included on the invoice:
  - On monthly invoices, you’re charged for all of your monthly seats for the month.
  - On annual subscription renewals, you’re charged for all of your annual seats for the next year.
- Seat adjustments or credit may be issued when you seat changes result in prorated credit for the unused time in the previous seat, monthly seats are converted into annual seats, or you have any prorated seat charges.
- Charges for any [additional AI credits on your plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/35865276858647).
- The total amount billed on the invoice, including breakdown of subtotal and taxes.
- Download a PDF of the invoice.

[Learn more about how billing works on the Professional plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034).

![Side by side of quarterly and annual invoices for the organization plan.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/38962247426839)

- The billing cadence and dates included in the invoice:
  - A quarterly invoice, which is sent every three months.
  - An annual renewal invoice, which is sent once a year.
- Invoice details, which include:
  - Due date
  - Invoice status
  - Invoice number, which you can click to view the invoice in Stripe
  - Payment method
- Seat charges included on the invoice:
  - On quarterly invoices, you’re charged for any additional seats you added to your account during the previous three months.
  - On annual subscription renewals, you’re charged for all of your seats for the next year.
- Seat credits may be issued when you change a user’s seat type, and the seat change results in prorated credit for the unused time in the previous seat.
- Charges for any [additional AI credits on your plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/35865276858647).
- The total amount billed on the invoice, including breakdown of subtotal and taxes.
- Download a PDF of the invoice.

[Learn more about how billing works on the Organization and Enterprise plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328293).

## Add a Tax ID to your invoice

**Note**: The option to add a VAT ID is only available to customers in [countries where Figma currently charges VAT or GST](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4418838088983).

Professional plan
Organization and Enterprise plans

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin**.
2. Click the **Settings** tab.
3. Under **Plan and Billing**, select **Value Added Tax / Goods and Services Tax.**
4. Enter your VAT ID.

1. Open the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183-Guide-to-the-file-browser) by logging in to your account at [figma.com](https://figma.com).
   - If you’re in a specific Figma file, you can access the file browser by clicking  > **Back to files** from the toolbar.
2. Select  **Admin.**
3. Click on the **Settings** tab.
4. Under **Billing**, select **Value Added Tax / Goods and Services Tax.**
5. Enter your VAT ID.

## Change payment method

Professional plan Organization and Enterprise plans

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin**.
2. Select the **Settings** tab.
3. In the **Billing** section, click **Payment details**.
4. Enter the new credit card or debit details in the fields provided.
5. Click **Save** to update.

**Note:** Figma supports payment by credit card or SEPA debit on the Professional plan. Payment via Paypal, ACH or wire transfer isn’t currently available.

5. Enter the new credit or debit card details in the fields provided.

## Update your company name and address on your invoice

If your shipping address is different from your billing address, you may wish to update the address we show on your invoices.

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin**.
2. Select the **Settings** tab.
3. In the **Billing** section, click **Invoice details**.
4. Enter the name and address you would like to appear on your invoice and press **Save**.

## Add custom information to your invoices

In some cases, you may need to add extra information to your invoices, such as a trading name or an additional company name.

If you need additional information to appear on your invoices, please [contact our support team](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/requests/new) and include the information you’d like to appear on your invoice. We'll do our best to accommodate your request.

## Add or update a billing contact

In Figma, billing contacts receive all billing updates and notifications.

Professional plan
Organization and Enterprise plans

By default, Figma sets the owner of the team as the billing contact. If you want billing reminders to go to someone else, you can update the billing contact. This person doesn’t need to have a Figma account.

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin**.
2. Select the **Settings** tab.
3. Under the **Billing notifications** heading, click **Billing contacts**.
4. Enter the email address in the field provided.
5. Click **Save** to update your billing contact.

Companies on the **Organization** or **Enterprise** plans can add multiple billing contacts. Figma sends billing notifications, such as notifications for true-ups or outstanding payments, to every billing contact.

You can add anyone's email address as the billing contact. This includes people without Figma accounts, as well as email aliases like [accounting@figma.com](mailto:accounting@figma.com). However, only organization admins can manage new paid seats or review quarterly true-ups.

To add or remove billing contacts:

1. Open the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183-Guide-to-the-file-browser) by logging in to your account at [figma.com](https://figma.com).
   - If you’re in a specific Figma file, you can access the file browser by clicking  > **Back to files** from the toolbar.
2. Select  **Admin** from the sidebar.
3. Select **Settings** at the top of the page.
4. Under the **Billing** section, click **Billing contacts**.
5. Add or remove email addresses for your billing contacts, separated by a comma.
6. Click **Save**.
