## 내용

Anyone can register for a Figma account using an email address and password, logging in with your Google account, or SAML SSO if you're a member of an organization.

Your account allows you access to everything in Figma, including your team or projects, files, and the Figma Community.

View and manage account-level settings, your Community settings, and notification settings.

1. Open Figma in the file browser.
2. Select your avatar  in the top-left corner to open the account menu.
3. Select **Settings** to open the settings modal.
4. Select **Account**, **Community**, or **Notifications**to explore available settings.
