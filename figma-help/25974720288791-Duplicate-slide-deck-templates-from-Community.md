## 내용

Before you Start

Who can use this feature

 

Available on [any plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273-Choose-a-Figma-Plan)

You need a [Figma account](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039811114-Create-a-Figma-account) to duplicate slide deck templates from Community

You can find slide deck templates that creators have shared with the Community and duplicate them to use in your own Figma Slides presentations. When you duplicate a template from Community, the template’s styles, colors, and slide layouts become available to use in your slide deck. Learn more about [using slide deck templates →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24242868379543-Create-a-new-slide-deck#h_01J0VDWV6BPYQZNCQH0WAYEGJ4)

Note: Slide deck templates published to Community are published under an Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) license. [Learn more →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360042296374-Figma-Community-copyright-and-licensing)

## Duplicate slide deck templates

When you duplicate a template, a new slide deck will be added to your drafts. This is an entirely new file, so you won’t be able to access any version history, comments, or permissions from the original slide deck.

Changes made to the original slide deck are not reflected in duplicated files. You must duplicate the updated file to view any changes.

1. On the slide deck template’s Community page, click **Use template**.
2. Select the account where you want to open the template.
3. A new slide deck opens in your drafts. Use the template picker to choose a specific slide layout or click **Add all slides**. Learn more about [using slide deck templates →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24242868379543-Create-a-new-slide-deck#h_01J0VDWV6BPYQZNCQH0WAYEGJ4)
