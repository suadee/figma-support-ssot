## 내용

Before you start

Who can use this feature

Supported on any team or plan.

You need a [Community profile](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360038510833-Create-a-Community-profile) to follow creators and save files. Plugins and widgets can be saved without a Community profile.

Show your support by following creators and saving your favorite files, plugins, and widgets.

You can't follow creators or comment when browsing Community as a team or organization admin. [Manage team and organization profiles →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4404108672663)

## Follow a creator, team, or organization

Follow individual creators, or follow a team or organization to stay in the loop with their creations.

You can follow creators from a few different places in the Community:

- **Search results:** On the **Creators** results page, click **Follow** next to a creator.
- **Community profile:** Click **Follow** underneath the creator's name.
- **Resource page:** Click the creator's name on the resource to open their profile, then click **Follow**.

## Save a resource

Save your favorite files, plugins, and widgets to quickly access them in the future.

To save a from Community resource, click  **Save** at the top of the resource’s Community page. You can also “like” a resource by clicking the heart button to show the creator your appreciation.

Note: You can also save plugins and widgets directly from a design file or FigJam board. Learn more about browsing resources in [Figma Design](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360042532714-Use-plugins-in-files#Run_plugins_from_the_Figma_Community) and [FigJam](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360042532714-Use-plugins-in-files#Run_plugins_from_FigJam).

## View and manage your saved resources

### Access saved files, plugins, and widgets from Community

If you have a [Community profile](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360038510833-Create-a-Community-profile), you can view your saved files, plugins, and widgets from the **Saved** tab of your profile. This tab is only visible to you.

Note: Saved files can only be accessed from the **Saved** tab of your Community profile. Saved plugins and widgets can be accessed from the **Saved** tab as well as Figma Design files and FigJam boards.

1. Navigate to your Community profile.
2. Select the **Saved** tab.

To remove a saved resource, click  **Remove from saves**.

### Access saved plugins and widgets from a Figma Design file

1. Click  **Resources** in the toolbar.
2. Select the **Plugins** or **Widgets** tab.

Your saved resources display under **Saved and recent** at the top of the modal.

Note: If you are using Dev Mode in Figma Design, your saved plugins display on the **Plugins** tab in the right sidebar under **Saved and recent**.

To remove a saved resource, click  **Remove from saves**.

### Access saved plugins and widgets from a FigJam board

1. Hover over the pile of icons in the toolbar and click **More**.
2. Select the **Widgets** or **Plugins** tab.

Your saved resources display under **Saved and recent**.

To remove a saved resource, click  **Remove from saves**.
