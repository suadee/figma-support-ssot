## 내용

In October 2025, Figma announced the acquisition of Weavy.

Now, Weavy is being reintroduced as Figma Weave. Here are some common questions about Figma Weave and what this means for you.

## What is Figma Weave?

Figma Weave is the new name for Weavy, a creative platform for AI-native media generation and editing.

Figma Weave is available today as a standalone experience at [weave.figma.com](https://weave.figma.com).

Over time, Figma Weave will integrate into the rest of the Figma platform. We’ll let you know as that happens.

## What is happening with Weavy?

Weavy has been acquired by Figma. The product and team are now **Figma Weave**.

To learn more, see our blog: [Introducing Figma Weave: the next generation of AI-native creation at Figma](https://www.figma.com/blog/welcome-weavy-to-figma/)

## What does this mean for me as an existing Figma or Figma Weave customer?

If you are a Figma customer, your experience with Figma remains the same. If you are a Figma Weave customer, your experience with Figma Weave remains the same.

Over time, Figma Weave’s AI-native workflows will be integrated into the Figma platform. For now, [users on paid Figma plans can run Weave tools in Figma Design](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/40779260614935).

We’ll share more information about further integration plans as soon as we can.

## When will Figma Weave be available in Figma?

A curated set of Weave tools is available in Figma Design for users on paid plans. You can browse and run these AI-powered tools on images in your files without leaving Figma.

[Learn how to use Weave tools in Figma](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/40779260614935).

Soon, you’ll be able to create and publish your own Weave tools—we’ll let you know when that is available.

Additionally, you can explore Figma Weave workflows from [Figma’s community page](https://www.figma.com/community/weavy). These are pre-built workflows to help get you started in Figma Weave quickly. They will redirect you to a Figma Weave file.

Otherwise, Figma and Figma Weave remain separate products at this time—you still need a Figma Weave account to use the full Figma Weave platform. You can go to [weave.figma.com](https://weave.figma.com/) and create an account for free. Learn more about using the Figma Weave platform from the [Figma Weave help center](https://help.weavy.ai/en/).

We’ll be sharing more details about Figma Weave in the coming months.

## How can I get started with Figma Weave?

You can start using Figma Weave today by signing up at [weave.figma.com](http://weave.figma.com). If you have an existing Figma account, you can use your Figma login credentials for your Figma Weave account.

To help you get started, explore Figma Weave workflows directly on [Figma’s community page](https://www.figma.com/community/weavy).

These are pre-built workflows you can use to get started quickly. When you select a Figma Weave community file, you will be redirected to Figma Weave where you can duplicate the file to your Figma Weave account and start exploring.

These files let you:

- Duplicate proven workflows into your workspace
- Learn how workflows are structured
- Customize them for your own creative needs

Learn more about using the Figma Weave platform from the [Figma Weave help center](https://help.weavy.ai/en/).

## What are best practices for using Figma Weave? How can I learn more?

Figma Weave is designed to help you build and scale creative workflows with AI.

For a deeper guide, check out our blog post on getting started with Figma Weave workflows, which walks through real examples and best practices: [Turning prompts into five scalable workflows with Figma Weave](https://www.figma.com/blog/five-figma-weave-workflows/)

Additionally, check out the [Figma Weave help center](https://help.weavy.ai/en/) or [Figma Weave Youtube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@figmaweave) to learn more.

## Are you sharing my data (AI training) or PII with Figma Weave?

No. Your personal data and any content used for AI features are not shared across platforms. We remain committed to privacy and data security—and will continue to be transparent about how data is used as we bring the platforms closer together.

## Why do I have to pay for both Figma and Figma Weave?

For now, Figma and Figma Weave are separate products with separate billing. We understand that’s not the most seamless setup. Over time, Figma Weave will be available directly within the Figma platform—but not yet.

## Will Figma Weave use my Figma AI credits?

No. Right now, Figma Weave AI credits and Figma AI credits are separate.

Because Figma Weave is not yet integrated into the Figma platform, each product manages its own AI usage and billing independently.

- [Learn more about Figma AI credits](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/33459875669015-How-AI-credits-work)
- [Learn more about Figma Weave AI credits](https://help.weavy.ai/en/articles/12267166-weavy-s-credit-system)

We’ll share more details about how credits may work in the future as integration progresses.

## How much will Figma Weave cost and what seats and plans will Figma Weave be available to?

We haven’t made any pricing changes yet. We’re still learning from users and figuring out how Figma Weave fits best within Figma’s plans. Expect more updates as we define our longer-term plans over the next year.
