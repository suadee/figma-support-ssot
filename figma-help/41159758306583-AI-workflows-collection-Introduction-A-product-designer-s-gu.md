## 내용

In this video, watch Rachel—a product designer at Cheddar, a budgeting app—use the Figma agent across different parts of her workflow: exploring multiple design directions early on, applying targeted edits across screens without losing momentum, and making sense of feedback after a review.

In the following articles, we'll take a deeper dive into each of these parts of the workflow and how to work with AI to get the best results:

- [Explore more directions, faster](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/41159787685143): prompt for divergence, and connect your design system
- [Cut down on busywork](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/41159765402007): make bulk edits and content updates across multiple screens
- [Get sharper feedback](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/41159752541719): synthesize comments and stress-test designs against other perspectives

**Tip:** To learn more about the capabilities of the Figma agent in design files—including a complete list of supported features—check out the [guide to working with the Figma agent in design files](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/37998629035799).
