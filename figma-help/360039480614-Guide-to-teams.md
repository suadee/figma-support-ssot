## 내용

Teams are designated spaces for projects and files. Use teams to collaborate with others, or to organize your work.

You can create or join multiple teams from a single Figma account. The way you create and join teams depends on your plan type:

You can create or join multiple teams from a single Figma account. The way you create and join teams depends on your plan type:

- Starter plan: The Starter plan is free and includes a single team. You can create one Starter team per account.
- Professional plan: The Professional plan includes a single team. You can have multiple teams on your account. Each team is managed separately.
- Organization and Enterprise plans: The Organization and Enterprise plans each include unlimited teams. Within an organization, you can create as many teams as you need. You can also join multiple teams within an organization, making it possible to work cross-functionally and share resources.

You can see a list of teams associated with your account from the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183-Guide-to-the-file-browser).

**Are you a student or educator?** You may be eligible for a free Education team.

[Learn more about Figma for Education](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061214-Verify-education-status).

## Who can manage a team?

In Figma, admins manage teams.

[Learn more about admins in Figma](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439-Admins-in-Figma).

### Starter and Professional teams

On the Starter or Professional plan, [team admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439-Admins-in-Figma) have the highest level of permissions. They can manage all aspects of a team, including team members and settings.

Additionally, team admins [manage billing on the Professional plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034-Manage-billing-on-the-Professional-plan).

### Teams in an organization

Organization admins Workspace admins Team admins

[Organization admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439-Admins-in-Figma) have the [highest level of access in an organization](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039829474). From  **Admin**, they can:

- Join any [visible teams](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039970673) as a team admin
- Manage team settings without joining the team, including:
  - Name
  - Access settings
  - Members and their permissions on the team
  - Icon
  - Description
- View the names of [hidden teams](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039970673)
- Join hidden teams as an owner only if the team has no owner and no team admins. If either exists, organization admins can't join.

[Workspace admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439-Admins-in-Figma) have the same permissions as organization admins, but their scope is limited to the workspaces where they are designated as admins.

[Learn about workspaces](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4409676189207).

A [team admin](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439-Admins-in-Figma) can manage all the settings of a single team, including people, resources, and access to the team within the organization. They can also delete the team.
