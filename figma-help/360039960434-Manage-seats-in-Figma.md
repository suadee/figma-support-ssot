## 내용

Who can use this feature

Available on the [Professional, Organization, and Enterprise plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273)

On the Professional plan, [team admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439) can manage seats.

On the Organization plan, [organization admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439) can manage seats.

On the Enterprise plan, [organization admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439) manage seats for anyone in the organization. [Billing group admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439) manage seats for people in billing groups they manage.

Every member and guest on paid Professional, Organization, or Enterprise plans has a seat type that determines which Figma products they can access.

Your plan and seat count affect your overall subscription cost.

**Want to learn more about billing and seats at Figma? Check out our related resources:**

- [Guide to billing at Figma](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/29717597009431)
- [Manage billing on the Professional plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034)
- [Manage billing on the Organization or Enterprise plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328293)

## How seats work in Figma

Figma has multiple products.

You assign a single seat for each user on your plan, and the seat type determines which products they have access to.

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Seat** | **What's included** |
| Full | - Full access to all Figma products, including Figma Design, Figma Make, Dev Mode, Figma Draw,  Figma Slides, and FigJam. |
| Dev | - Full access to Dev Mode, Figma Slides, and FigJam. - View and comment access in Figma Design files. - Does not include access to [design mode in Figma Slides](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/25423848723863-Use-design-mode-in-Figma-Slides). |
| Collab | - Full access to Figma Slides and FigJam. - View and comment access in Figma Design files. - No access to Dev Mode; [basic inspection](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/22012921621015-Guide-to-inspecting) only. - Does not include access to [design mode in Figma Slides](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/25423848723863-Use-design-mode-in-Figma-Slides). |
| View | - View and comment access to Figma Design, Figma Slides, and FigJam. - No access to Dev Mode; [basic inspection](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/22012921621015-Guide-to-inspecting) only. |

Note: Figma has additional products, such as Figma Sites or Figma Buzz, that are currently in beta. [Learn more about access to beta features](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4406787442711).

To check the current price per seat for your plan, visit [figma.com/pricing](https://figma.com/pricing).

A user’s [permissions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500007609322-Guide-to-sharing-and-permissions) determine which files they have access to within each product.

## Manage user seats

When you purchase a Figma plan, you purchase a set number of seats for your billing term. These seats are available for you to assign to users as you see fit.

### Change seat type

1. From the file browser, click  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select the  **People** tab.
3. Click a person’s current seat from the **Seat type** column.
4. In the popup modal, select and confirm the new seat type.

![Change a users seat type by selecting their current seat from the Seat type column.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/30567574117399)

[Learn more about how seat changes work](#how-seat-changes-work).

### Add new seats

New seats can be added to your subscription as new users join your team or organization. When you invite users, you can choose which seat type to give them.

- [Invite team members](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039481034-Invite-team-members)
- [Add members or guests to an organization](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040453113-Add-members-or-guests-to-an-organization)

On the Professional plan, you can also [purchase additional seats](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034#add-seats-on-annual) at any time.

Members can also [request seats](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040453433). Admins [set approval settings for new seats](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4414038570007), which determine if new seat requests are manually or automatically approved.

Note: Additional approved seats are added to your next invoice. If you’re on the annual Professional plan, new seats are billed on a separate monthly subscription.

[Learn more about billing on the Professional plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034).

[Learn more about billing on the Organization or Enterprise plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328293).

### Remove seats

Seats are purchased for the duration of your billing term. Before your next renewal date, you’ll have the chance to remove any unused seats from your account from your **Billing** overview page.

If you need to remove a user’s access prior to your next renewal, you can either:

- Change their seat to the free View seat, which provides only view and comment across Figma products.
- [Remove the user from the team](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039965493-Remove-access-to-teams) (Professional plan) or [remove the user from the organization](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040453453-Remove-people-from-an-organization) (Organization and Enterprise plans).

In both scenarios, their previous seat is made available for someone else to use for the remainder of the subscription period.

You can remove available (unassigned) seats at your next plan renewal.

- For annual plans, thirty days before your annual plan is set to renew, you’ll see a banner at the top of your Figma admin. Click **Review seats** to open a modal to adjust the number of seats on your plan for your next renewal.
- For monthly Professional plans, there is no renewal period. You can make changes to seats on a monthly subscription at any time. Unassigned seats will not be renewed for the next month.

Note: If your organization purchased Figma through an order form, your renewal experience may differ. You’ll see your renewal banner 60 days before renewal to review seats, but you may not be able to reduce seats directly in Figma admin. Changes like decreasing seat count may require working with your Figma account team and issuing a new order form, and must typically be completed before the 30-day renewal period.

[Learn more about billing on the Organization and Enterprise plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328293).

[Learn more about how seat changes work](#how-seat-changes-work).

### Available seats

A seat is available if it has been purchased in advance, but no one is currently assigned to it. For example:

- When someone leaves your team or organization, their seat will become available for someone else to use.
- If an admin moves someone to a free View seat, their current seat becomes available.
- If you’ve pre-purchased seats at the beginning of a subscription term, but no one is using them yet.

You are billed for all seats on your plan, whether they are available (unassigned) or unavailable (assigned to a user). On the [Professional plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034), monthly seats that are unassigned are automatically removed at the next monthly invoice.

Here’s how to see how many available seats you have on your plan:

1. From the file browser, click  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select the  **Billing** tab.

In the **Overview** tab, you'll see the total number of seats on your plan, and how many seats are available, if any.

Tip: You can also view how many seats you have at the top of the **People** page.

### How seat changes work

Approving seat requestsMoving users to free View seatsMoving users to lower cost seats

When an admin approves a seat request, we first check to see whether the requested seat is available in your plan. A seat is available if it is not assigned to a user.

- **If the requested seat is available**: The seat is assigned to the user, and their previous seat is made available for someone else to use. This results in no additional seats or costs.
- **If the requested seat is not available**: You need to add an additional seat to your subscription.

To avoid being charged for seats you no longer need, review and adjust your seat count before your next plan renewal.

Learn more about how seat changes impact billing and how to remove unused seats:

- [Manage billing on the Professional plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034)
- [Manage billing on the Organization and Enterprise plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328293)

Example: A user with a Collab seat requests a Full seat

John has requested a seat change from a Collab to a Full seat.

When you approve the request, you can see if you have any Full seats available.

- If a Full seat is available, John can start using it, and their Collab seat is made available for someone else to use.
- If there is no Full seat available, Figma will add a Full seat to your subscription.

An admin can move a user out of their assigned seat to a free View seat at any time. The user's existing seat will become unassigned and available for someone else to use.

When a user is moved from a paid seat to a View seat, they will no longer be able to edit files. Any draft files owned by that user will no longer be accessible to other collaborators unless they are [moved to a shared project](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360038511573) or [transferred to another user](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360038512093-Transfer-ownership-of-files-or-projects) before the downgrade.

To avoid being charged for seats you no longer need, review and adjust your seat count before your next plan renewal.

Learn more about how seat changes impact billing and how to remove unused seats:

- [Manage billing on the Professional plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034)
- [Manage billing on the Organization and Enterprise plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328293)

If an admin wants to move a user to a lower cost seat, we first check to see whether that lower cost seat is available in your plan. A seat is available if it is not assigned to a user.

- **If the seat is available**: The seat is assigned to the user, and their previous seat is made available for someone else to use. This results in no additional seats or costs.
- **If the seat is not available**: You need to purchase the desired seat for this user, and their existing seat will be made available for someone else to use.

To avoid being charged for seats you no longer need, review and adjust your seat count before your next plan renewal.

Learn more about how seat changes impact billing and how to remove unused seats:

- [Manage billing on the Professional plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034)
- [Manage billing on the Organization and Enterprise plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328293)

Example: An admin moves a user from a Full seat to a Collab seat

An admin is reviewing seat usage and decides to move John from a Full seat to a Collab seat, in order to make his Full seat available for another use.

When reviewing seats, you can see if you have any Collab seats available.

- If a Collab seat is available, John will get assigned to it and their Full seat is made available for someone else to use. This results in no additional seats or costs.
- If there is no Collab seat available, Figma will add one to your subscription and make John’s Full seat available for someone else to use. If you don’t need the seat any more, you can remove it at your next renewal.

## Manage seat requests

Members or guests can [request seats](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040453433) for Figma products they do not have access to. As an admin, you can use seat approval settings to automatically manage new seat requests.

For each seat type, admins can choose from the following [seat approval settings](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4414038570007):

- Manually approve seats
- Manually approve, unless seat is available
- Auto-approve seats

[Review pending seat requests](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500003870721) from your admin **Dashboard**.
