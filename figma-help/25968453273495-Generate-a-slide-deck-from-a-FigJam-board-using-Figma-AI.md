After hosting an engaging team brainstorm or user research session in FigJam, you may have a lot of content to sift through before you can share your findings in a Figma Slides deck. With Figma AI, you can generate a slide deck from a FigJam board, giving you a good starting point that you can use to build the rest of your presentation.

## Generate a slide deck

You can generate a slide deck from any FigJam board you have `can view` access to. There are a few ways to generate a slide deck:

### From Figma Slides

1. Click **New slide** in the left sidebar to open the template picker.
2. Click **Make Slides with an outline**.
3. Choose a FigJam board to summarize.
4. If the FigJam board you selected has multiple pages, use the dropdown menu to select a specific page or choose **All pages** to summarize the entire board.
5. Click **Next**.
6. Select a template to use for the deck or click **Create deck without template**.

Figma AI sorts and summarizes the content on the FigJam and generates a slide deck. You can modify the generated slides and add additional slides to the deck, as needed.

### From FigJam

1. Open the board you want to use.
2. Do one of the following:
   - Go to  **Main menu**> **File**> **Turn FigJam board into deck**
   - Click **More actions** next to a page and select **Turn FigJam board into deck**
   - Click **Share** in the top toolbar and select **Turn FigJam board into deck**
   - Select sticky notes on the board, right-click and select **Turn FigJam board into deck**
3. You’ll be redirected to the template picker in Figma Slides. Select a template to use for the deck or click **Create deck without template**.

Figma AI sorts and summarizes the content on the FigJam board and generates a slide deck. You can modify the generated slides and add additional slides to the deck, as needed.
