## My bill is higher than expected

### What happened?

If your bill is higher than expected, it's probable that more paid seats have been added to your Figma plan than initially anticipated.

When an admin approves a seat request, we first check to see whether the requested seat is available in your plan. A seat is available if it is not assigned to a user.

- **If the requested seat is available**: The seat is assigned to the user, and their previous seat is made available for someone else to use. This results in no additional seats or costs.
- **If the requested seat is not available**: You need to purchase the requested seat.

[Learn more about managing seats at Figma](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039960434).

### How to fix it

You can review your seats at any time during your billing cycle.

If you want to restrict someone's access, you can downgrade them to a free View seat.

Additionally, you can change your seat approval settings to a more restrictive setting to ensure that new seats are only getting added if they’ve been explicitly approved by an admin.

Note: We won’t issue a prorated refund when you downgrade a user to a free View seat, or remove the seat. However, someone else at your company can use the paid seat for the remainder of the billing period. If you no longer need the seat, make that adjustment at your next plan renewal.

**Change someone's seat type**

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select the  **People** tab.
3. Click a person’s seat in the **Seat type** column.
4. In the popup modal, select and confirm the View seat.

![Admin panel showing user list with seat types and billing groups, highlighting seat type selection.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/30567574437655)

[Learn more about managing seats](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039960434).

**Adjust seats at renewal**

At your plan renewal, you’ll be able to adjust the number of seats on your subscription.

[Learn more about managing billing on the Professional plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034).

[Learn more about managing billing on the Organization or Enterprise plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328293).

**Change seat approval settings**

For each seat type, admins can choose from the following seat approval settings:

- **Manually approve seats**: All seat upgrade requests must be manually approved by an admin.
- **Manually approve, unless seat is available** (default): People who request a paid seat are automatically granted one if it is available.
- **Auto-approve seats**: All seat upgrade requests are automatically granted. If there are no seats available, a new seat will be purchased.

To adjust your seat approval setting:

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select the  **Settings** tab.
3. In the **Billing** section, click **Seat approval settings**.
4. In the popup modal, select an approval setting for each seat type.

![Seat approval settings highlighted in billing panel of the admin, showing options to configure how seat requests and upgrades are approved.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/30567583818391)

## I am being charged monthly on an annual subscription

### What happened?

Your annual subscription covers the number of Full, Dev, and Collab seats you had when you first upgraded your team.

If you are on the Professional plan and add more paid seats to your team, Figma will charge you for those seats on a separate monthly subscription. This gives you the flexibility to manage seats on multiple billing cadences.

[Learn more about billing on the Professional plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034).

### How to fix it

If new users only need access to paid features for a short period of time, you can keep managing their billing on a monthly cadence. You can then move them to a View seat when their access is no longer needed.

Downgrade or remove their access

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin**.
2. Select the  **People** tab.
3. Click a person’s seat in the **Seat type** column.
4. In the popup modal, select and confirm the View seat.

Unassigned seats will not be renewed for the next month.

If you’d like those users to be a part of your annual subscription, you can simplify your billing by converting them to annual seats.

Add new seats to your annual subscription

If you’d like those users to be a part of your annual subscription, you can simplify your billing by converting them to annual users. Figma will charge you a pro-rated fee for the remainder of the billing period.

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin**.
2. Select  **Settings**.
3. In the Plan section, select **Add additional seats**.
4. Use the  plus and  minus buttons to adjust the number of of seats you want to add. You can add seats for each seat type separately.
5. Click **Add annual seats**.
