## 내용

Figma partners with innovative companies and service providers to make the product development process more collaborative and efficient.

## Become a Figma partner

Learn more about the types of partnerships available on [Figma’s Partner Program page.](https://www.figma.com/partners/)

If you think you’d be a great fit, fill out one of the following applications:

- [Apply to be a product integration partner](https://wkf.ms/3XwyJHH)
- [Apply to be a service partner](https://wkf.ms/3OYDsPL)

If your application is deemed to be eligible for partnership, the Figma team will reach out via email.

## Work with a Figma service partner

Figma’s service partners can help you or your organization with various tasks, including:

- Implementing and training on Figma
- Creating and managing design systems
- Migrating to Figma or FigJam
- Building digital experiences together

Learn more about working with a [Figma service partner](http://figma.com/partners/find-a-service-partner).

If you like to connect with a Figma service partner, fill out this [interest form](https://forms.monday.com/forms/357729d55ca6f7c5dbe8c84703a37c02?r). We will pass along your information to the applicable service partner. If interested in a partnership, the service partner will contact you directly via email.

## Figma partner badges

Partner badges  are badges that show up on select profiles in the [Figma Community](https://www.figma.com/community/partners).

The partner badge is only given to creators or company profiles who are verified Figma partners.
