## 내용

Before you start

Who can use this feature

Supported on the [Organization and Enterprise plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273).

Any member of an organization can publish a private widget.

Only organization admins can [publish public widgets to an organization profile](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4410337103639).

Organizations often have internal tools and systems, with private information or data, that can't be shared outside the organization.

Private widgets allow members of the organization to use organization-specific widgets, without having to share them with the rest of the Community.

- Any member of an organization can make and share a private widget.
- Only team admins can publish widgets to the team's Community profile.
- Only organization admins can share publish widgets to the organization's Community profile.

## Publish private widgets

When [publishing a widget](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4410337103639), you can use the **Publish to** setting to choose whether to publish the widget to the organization or the Community. Private widgets don't need to go through the review process before they are available to use.

![](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/34104197161239)

To create a private widget for an organization, follow the steps to [publish widgets to the Figma Community](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4410337103639). On **Add the final details** page, set **Publish to** to your organization.

Only the widget's original publisher can change the plugin's access. They can update this setting by publishing an update to the widget.

Update the **Publish to** setting to change who can access the widget. If you're changing a widget from private to public, it will need to go through Figma's plugin and widget review process.

[Learn how to publish widgets →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4410337103639)

If the original publisher has left the organization and you need to transfer ownership of a private widget, [contact the Figma Support team](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041057214).

## View private widgets

To find and install widgets that are private to your organization:

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click **All teams** or **All workspaces**.
2. Select the **Widgets** tab at the top of the page.
3. Browse through any widgets that are private to your organization.
4. Select a widget and click the **↓ Try it out** button to add the widget to your account.
