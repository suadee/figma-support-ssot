## 내용

Who can use this feature

Available on the [Enterprise plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273)

[Organization admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439) only

Deleting a workspace will remove the workspace from your organization. When you delete a workspace, the people and teams in the workspace will move to **Unassigned**. This process doesn’t delete any files or projects.

To delete a workspace:

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), select **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select the **Content** tab.
3. Click the **Workspaces** tab.
4. Click next to the workspace and select **Delete workspace**.
5. Check the box next to **I understand that this action isn't reversible**.
6. Click **Delete workspace** to apply.

**Note**: It's not possible to restore a deleted workspace. However, you can create a new workspace using the same name, workspace admin(s), and members.

Once you’ve deleted your workspace, you can reassign affected members, guests and teams to another workspace.

- **[Assign people to workspaces →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4419365500695)**
- **[Assign teams to workspaces →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4419471354007)**
