## 내용

Figma has several products: [Figma Design](https://www.figma.com/design/), [Dev Mode](https://www.figma.com/dev-mode/), [FigJam](https://www.figma.com/figjam/), [Figma Slides](https://www.figma.com/slides/), and [Figma Make](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304412302231). You can choose which products to use on each of our four plans. Figma has additional products, such as Figma Sites or Figma Buzz, that are currently in beta. [Learn more about access to beta features](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4406787442711).

**Tip**: Visit the [pricing page](https://figma.com/pricing) for detailed pricing information about each plan.

**Tip**: Have specific needs for your plan? Explore these additional offerings:

- [**Figma for Government**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/22932461365015): Built for government agencies, with enhanced security and compliance controls.
- [**Governance+**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31825370509591) (Enterprise add-on): Advanced admin and security features for greater control and visibility.
- [**Figma for Education**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061214): Free access for eligible students and educators, designed for classroom use.
