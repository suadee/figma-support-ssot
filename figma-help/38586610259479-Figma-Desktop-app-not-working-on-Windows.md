## 내용

If the Figma Desktop app on Windows is showing a black screen, it's usually due to a compatibility issue between your computer’s graphics card and the app. You may be able to fix the problem by disabling the **Prefer High-Performance GPU** option.

To do so:

1. Right-click the Figma icon in your system tray.
2. Go to Troubleshooting > uncheck the **Prefer High-Performance GPU** option.

If you don’t see the Figma icon in your system tray, [clear the Figma desktop app cache](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/22380853110551) and try again.
