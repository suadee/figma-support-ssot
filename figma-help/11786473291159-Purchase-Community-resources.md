## 내용

You can purchase resources from creators on the Figma Community.

Resources on the Community may be sold using Figma’s payment platform or through a third-party platform. Figma is unable to provide purchase or refund support for resources sold on third-party platforms. You’ll need to contact the creator directly if you need assistance.

Note: We are unable to accept payments from users with Russian or Indian-based credit cards at this time.

## Purchase paid files and templates

You can preview a paid file before purchasing it. To preview a file, navigate to the file’s Community page and click **Get free preview**.

The checkout process differs depending on the payment platform used. You can identify if the file is sold using Figma’s payment platform or a third-party platform from the file’s Community page:

![Comparison of Figma (A) and third-party (B) payment buttons; A shows price, B redirects offsite.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/29908421040535)

**A.** This file is sold through Figma’s payment platform. The price is displayed on the **Buy** button.

**B.** This file is sold through a third-party platform. Clicking the **Buy** button will redirect you to the third-party site where you can view more information and purchase the file.

To purchase a file sold through Figma’s payment platform:

1. From the file’s Community, click **Buy**.
2. Enter your payment details.
3. Click **Complete purchase**.
4. From the file’s Community page, click **Open** to access the file.

Resource not what you expected? You can request an [automatic refund](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/12816440381975) within 24 hours of purchasing a resource from the Community.

## Purchase paid plugins and widgets

Paid plugins and widgets include a free trial so you can preview the resource before purchasing. To start your free trial, click **Try in** on the resource’s Community page and select which file you’d like to add the resource to.

Once your free trial ends, you’ll be prompted to purchase the resource. The checkout process differs depending on the payment platform used. For resources sold using Figma’s payment platform, you’ll be prompted to purchase the resource directly in the current file. For resources sold using third-party platforms, you’ll be prompted to go to that site to purchase the resource.

![Trial ended message for a Figma plugin, prompting purchase for $5/month with options to subscribe or defer.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/29908421047831)

To purchase a plugin or widget sold through Figma’s payment platform:

1. After your free trial ends, click **Buy** (one-payment) or **Subscribe**(monthly subscription).
2. Enter your payment details.
3. Click **Complete purchase**.
4. From the resource’s Community page, click **Open in** to access the resource.

Resource not what you expected? You can request an [automatic refund](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/12816440381975) within 24 hours of purchasing a resource from the Community.

## Sales tax and VAT

Figma Community purchases are taxed based on your address. When purchasing your first Community resource, you’ll be prompted to add your payment details and address. Your address will be saved to your Community profile for future purchases.

To update your address or add a VAT ID:

1. Open Figma in the file browser.
2. Select your avatar in the top-left corner to open the account menu.
3. Click **Settings**.
4. Select the **Community** tab.
5. Scroll to the Address section to view your current address details.
6. Select **Update address** to change your billing address or to add a VAT ID.

### Value-added tax (VAT)

If applicable, VAT/GST charges will also be added to your invoice in the following countries. You can can provide your VAT/GST ID (or applicable tax ID) to ensure correct tax calculations.

- European Union (EU)
- Japan
- Liechtenstein and Switzerland
- Mexico
- South Africa
- Russia
- United Kingdom (UK)

You can update your billing details to include a VAT ID from your account settings under the **Community** tab.

## View your purchased resources

You’ll find your purchased resources on the **Community** tab of your account settings. From there, you can view order details, contact the resource creator for support, or [request a refund](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/12816440381975).

Note: Paid resources are not accessible on the Figma iOS apps. You can still access your paid resources on mobile web browsers.

To view your purchased resources:

1. Open Figma in the file browser.
2. Select your avatar in the top-left corner to open the account menu.
3. Click **Settings**.
4. Select the **Community** tab.
5. Scroll to Community purchases to view your purchase history.

Note: If you purchased a plugin subscription using Figma’s
payment platform, you can
[manage your subscriptions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/29316602010775-Manage-your-plugin-subscriptions)
from your Stripe customer portal. If the price increases on an active
subscription, you will be notified by email and an in-product notification
at least 16 days before the increase takes effect. We won’t notify you
if the price of a subscription decreases.
