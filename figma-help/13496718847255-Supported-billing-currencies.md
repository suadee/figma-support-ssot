## 내용

Starting **April 2023,** you can pay for new **Professional,** **Organization** or **Enterprise** plan subscriptions using the following currencies:

- British pounds (GBP)
- Canadian dollars (CAD)
- Euros (EUR)
- Japanese yen (JPY)
- U.S. dollars (USD)

**Note**: You can change the billing currency of an Organization or Enterprise plan by reaching out to your sales representative. After the billing currency is changed, we automatically update your credit balance based on current exchange rates. Your next invoice is billed in the new selected currency.
