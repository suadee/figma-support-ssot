## 내용

The Starter plan is a free Figma plan. The Starter plan might be right for you if you:

- Are working on personal projects
- Need basic design, presentation, and brainstorming tools
- Want to try Figma products

Want to compare the Starter plan to Figma’s paid plans? [Learn more about Figma’s plans and pricing](https://www.figma.com/pricing/).

## Products and features

On the Starter plan, you can access the following Figma products:

- [Figma Design](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/categories/360002042553)
- [FigJam](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/categories/360002051633)
- [Figma Slides](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24170630629911-Explore-Figma-Slides)
- [Figma Draw](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31440394517143-Explore-Figma-Draw)
- [Figma Sites](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31230436657815-Explore-Figma-Sites)
- [Figma Buzz](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31271566667543-Guide-to-Figma-Buzz)
- [Figma Make](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304412302231-Explore-Figma-Make)

[Dev Mode](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/15023124644247-Guide-to-Dev-Mode) is not available on the Starter plan.

Additionally, there is limited feature availability within in each product on the Starter plan. For example:

- No [team libraries](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041051154-Guide-to-libraries-in-Figma)
- Only 30 days [version history](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360038006754-View-a-file-s-version-history)
- No [publishing in Figma Sites](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31242845959703-Publish-update-or-unpublish-a-site)
- No [template publishing in Figma Slides](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24290529512471-Publish-slide-deck-templates-to-a-team-or-organization) or [Figma Buzz](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31271693614487-Create-and-publish-Figma-Buzz-templates)
- No [SSO](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/sections/4403936348311) or [advanced security settings](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/sections/20833164749719)

To better understand which individual features are available on the Starter plan, you can:

- Review Figma’s [pricing and plans page](https://www.figma.com/pricing/)
- Review the “Who can use this feature” box at the top of each Figma Learn article

## Starter plan structure

![A graphic depicting Starter team structure. Files sit within a project, which sits within a team.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/35395606169623)

The Starter plan includes a single team and project.

Within the project, Starter teams are limited to:

- 3 total Figma Design and Figma Sites files
- 3 FigJam files
- 3 Figma Slides files

Note: There is currently no limit to the number of Figma Buzz files you can have on the starter plan.

Additionally, while there's no file limit for Figma Make on the Starter plan, your usage is limited by your monthly AI credit allowance. [Learn more about how AI credits work](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/33459875669015).

On the Starter plan, you can have an unlimited number of drafts. Drafts are files that have not been moved into a project.

### Starter plan permissions

You can share different kinds of resources in Figma: files, prototypes, projects and teams.

When you share a resource in Figma, you set permissions to `can edit` or `can view`. Permissions determine how a user can interact with the resource.

On the Starter team, keep in mind the following specifications when it comes to sharing and permissions:

- Team members can access the project based on their team-level permissions. If you want to make projects with `can view` access or have projects that are only open to those invited, you need to [upgrade to a paid plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360046216313).
- On the Starter plan, you can only invite collaborators to drafts with `can view` access. If you want to invite collaborators to your draft files with edit access, you need to [upgrade to a paid plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360046216313).

[Learn more about sharing and permissions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500007609322-Guide-to-sharing-and-permissions).
