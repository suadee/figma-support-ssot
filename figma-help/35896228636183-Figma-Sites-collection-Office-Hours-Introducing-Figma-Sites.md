## About this video

In this replay from our June 24, 2025 webinar, you'll learn more about how you can design, prototype, and publish websites all in Figma Sites.

Join the Figma team who will walk through the latest features including:

- Templates and blocks
- Code layers (launched just prior to this recording)
- Preview and publish your site
- Best practices for making your website responsive
- and ways to add interactions and animations

Stay tuned to the end of this video to view the audience Q&A session and hear directly from the Figma Product team.
