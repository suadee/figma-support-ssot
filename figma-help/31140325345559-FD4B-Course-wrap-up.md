Congratulations on completing the Figma Design for beginners course!

We've covered so many things during our time together. If you’re looking to keep practicing and growing your skills, visit the [Figma Help Center](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us), where you’ll find a library of resources, including:

- [Hands-on projects](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/sections/13148624530967)

And don’t forget to check out Figma’s [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCQsVmhSa4X-G3lHlUtejzLA) for more video tutorials and tips. Make sure to subscribe to stay up-to-date with new tutorials or updates and additions to this course.

Thank you for joining us on this learning journey. We can’t wait to see what you’ll make next!
