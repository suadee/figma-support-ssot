## 내용

Who can use this feature

Available on the [Enterprise plan.](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273)

Both members and guests can be assigned to a workspace. Only members can browse or select a workspace.

[Workspaces](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4409676189207) are groups of teams, people, and resources within an organization. They could be groups, departments, product areas, or business units.

Organization admins can allow members to select their own workspace. You'll usually only need to do this once. You can still browse teams, libraries and resources from other workspaces.

1. When you next log in, you'll see a **Select workspace** window:
2. Next to **Workspace:** click **Select your workspace** .

   - Select the workspace that suits.
   - If your workspace isn't listed, select **My workspace isn't listed**.
   - If you're not sure which workspace you belong to, select **I don't know my workspace**.

   ![Dropdown menu showing workspace options for selection.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/4419365265303)
3. Click **Submit** to select your workspace. Figma will take you to the organization workspace you selected.

**Note:** You can also reach out to an organization admin to check which workspace you should select. You can find a list of **Admins** on the organization page in Figma.
