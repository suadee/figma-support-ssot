## 내용

AI credits are used to run AI actions in Figma.

On the Education plan, AI credits:

- Are assigned to each person (they are not shared across teammates)
- Reset monthly and don’t roll over

Education plan users get 3,000 AI credits per month. Once you hit your credit limit, you are unable to use AI features that require credits until your limit resets, or you move to a plan that gives you more options for additional credits.

Tip: To learn more about how AI credits work or the Education plan, see:

- [How AI credits work](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/33459875669015-How-AI-credits-work)
- [Figma for Education](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061214-Figma-for-Education)

## Check your AI credit balance

You can view your remaining AI credits at any time.

1. Open the  **Main menu** in any file.
2. Select the **AI balance** option to see how many credits you’ve used, how many credits you have remaining, and when they’ll reset.

![A user checks their AI credit balance from the file menu inside a Figma file.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/37669362664215)

## How to get more AI credits

If you reach your monthly credit limit, you won’t be able to run additional AI actions until one of the following happens:

- Your credits reset on your next monthly reset date, or
- You move to a plan that gives you more options for additional credits

Upgrading to a paid Professional team can help because:

- Upgrading resets your credit limit, giving you a fresh set of included credits. On the Professional plan, Full seats include 3,000 credits per month. Other seats include 500 credits per month.
- Paid plans can [purchase additional AI credits](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/35865276858647).

## Upgrade process for Education users

There are two ways to move from the Education plan to a paid Professional plan. The right option depends on whether you need to keep collaborating with other Education users.

- If you want to keep your Education team for collaboration, create a new, separate Professional team and move specific files to it.
- If you work solo and don’t need to collaborate with other Education users, you can upgrade your existing Education team to a paid Professional plan.

### Create a new team

To create a new, paid Professional plan team:

1. Visit <https://www.figma.com/files/create-team> to create a new team.
2. Upgrade that team to the Professional plan.
3. Move any file(s) to the new Professional team.

[Learn more about creating new teams](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039484194-Create-a-new-team).

You can keep your Education team active for collaboration with other Education users and move only the files you want to continue working on with AI to your Professional team.

[Learn more about moving files](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360038511573-Move-a-file).

If you belong to multiple teams, you can switch between them by clicking the team name in the file browser sidebar.

![The left sidebar of the file browser, where you can click your team name to switch between teams your account is associated with.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/37669362667671)

### Upgrade your existing Education team

If you don’t need to collaborate with other Education users, you may prefer to upgrade your existing Education team directly to a paid Professional team.

Before you upgrade, keep in mind:

- You can’t mix free Education members and paid non-Education members on the same Professional team.
- After upgrading, other Education members won’t be able to collaborate on that team for free.

To upgrade your team to a paid plan, see [Upgrade or downgrade your plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360046216313-Upgrade-or-downgrade-your-plan).

Note: If you’d like to change from a Professional team back to an Education team, you’ll need to [downgrade your plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360046216313-Upgrade-or-downgrade-your-plan) to the free Starter plan. At the end of your billing period, Figma will downgrade your team to a free Starter team. You can then [upgrade your Starter team to an Education team again](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061214-Figma-for-Education).
