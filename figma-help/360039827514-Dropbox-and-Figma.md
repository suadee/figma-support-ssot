## 내용

[Dropbox Paper](https://www.dropbox.com/paper) is an online workspace, where you can organize text, media, and files. Paper lets you collaborate with others and access your Paper docs from anywhere.

[Figma Live Embeds](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039827134-Figma-Live-Embed) allow you to add live Figma files or prototypes into other websites or apps. Add Figma links to your Dropbox Paper documents to:

- Embed Figma files inside your meeting notes or design specs.
- Share your designs with teammates.
- Collect design feedback from collaborators.

# Embed a file or prototype in Dropbox

You can embed any file or prototype, regardless of the file's [link sharing settings](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040531773).

Collaborators can interact with Figma Live Embeds based on:

- Their [team](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039970673) or [Organization permissions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039960434)
- The file's [link sharing permissions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040531773-Share-Files-with-anyone-using-Link-Sharing#Update_Link_Sharing)

## In Figma

1. Open the file you want to embed in your Paper doc.
2. To link to a specific frame in the Figma file, select the frame.
3. Click the **Share** button in the toolbar.
4. Update your [link sharing permissions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040531773-Share-Files-with-anyone-using-Link-Sharing#Update_Link_Sharing) to determine who can view and interact with the file embed (Optional).
5. Click **Copy link** when you're ready to embed the file.

## In Dropbox

1. Open the Paper doc you want to embed the file or prototype in.
2. Click on an empty space in the doc.
3. Click **Add Media** from the toolbar that appears at the bottom-center of the Paper doc.  
   ![Dropbox Paper toolbar with option to add media.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/360070622894)
4. Select Figma from the apps list.  
   ![Dropbox Paper interface showing Figma selected for embedding, with a prompt to paste a Figma link.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/360071740253)
5. Paste the link for your Figma file or prototype into the field, then press `Enter` / `Return` to apply the embed.  
   ![Dropbox Paper interface showing the option to embed a Figma file with a URL input field and a preview pane.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/360071740333)
6. You can then interact with the file or prototype. Learn more in our [Interact with live embeds](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360051741274) article.

![Dropbox Paper document with an embedded Figma file showing a 3D table animation with various reaction emojis.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/360070614994)

Tip! You can also paste a Figma link directly into a Paper doc. Dropbox will then turn this into an interactive embed for you.
