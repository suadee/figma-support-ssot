## 내용

Before you start

Who can use this feature

 

Available on [any team or plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273-Choose-a-Figma-Plan)

There are three ways to register or authenticate your account in Figma: email address and unique password, Google SSO, and SAML SSO. Your sign up method will dictate whether you can update your email address and password in Figma.

## Email address and password

If you signed up for Figma with your email address and a unique password, you can update both your email and password in your account settings.

Note: If you no longer have access to the email address you used to create your Figma account, contact [Figma Support](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041057214-Get-help-with-Figma#h_01J5V2MSWKPTK0Z9A4MCYBD1AM) for assistance.

1. Open the file browser and click your profile in the top-left corner to open the account menu.
2. Select **Settings** to open the settings modal.
3. Select the **Account** tab to update your details
4. Click **Change email** underneath your email address
5. Click **Change password** to reset your password.
6. Click **Save** to apply your changes.

## Google SSO

When you sign up for Figma via Google SSO, your email address and password will be **Managed by Google**. This means that you won't be able to update your email address or password in Figma.

If you want to change your Google email address or password, you will need to do this in your Google account. For detailed instructions, follow the Google Support links below:

- [Change or reset your password](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/41078?co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop&hl=en)
- [Change your username or email address](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/19870?hl=en&co=GENIE.Platform=Android)

### Switch from Google SSO to email and password

If your email address and password are **Managed by Google**, you can't update them in Figma. If you want to change the email associated with your account, you'll need to remove Google SSO.

To remove Google SSO, you need to request and set a new account password. This allows you log in with your email address and unique password. Once updated, you can change the email to any address.

1. Log out of your Figma account.
2. Visit the [Figma website](https://www.figma.com/) and click **Log in** in the top-right corner.
3. Click the **Reset password** link.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to request a password reset email.
5. Click the link in your password reset email. Figma will prompt you to enter a new password.
6. When you next go to log in, you can log in via your gmail address and your new password.
7. If you want to change the email address associated with your account, you can now [update your email address in Figma.](#h_01EHAPMPERQ5HJMQTWR713Q9GV)

## SAML SSO

If you log in to Figma with your company email address via SAML SSO, your organization's[identity provider](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040532333) will manage your login details.

We recommend contacting your IT department, or an organization admin, for help updating your email address or password there. You can still [update your name and profile picture](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041034433) in Figma.
