## 내용

In Figma files, you can minimize the UI or hide the entire UI:

- [Minimizing](#toggle) the left and right UI in Figma allows you to view more of the canvas while still having access to properties in the right sidebar when you select a layer.
- [Hiding](#h_01J13AA0KJBTDP3KAPWTYNSB7C) the entire UI will conceal the navigation bar, left and right sidebars, and the toolbar. This is great for maximizing the amount of space in your window for viewing designs.

### Minimize the UI

To **Minimize** or **Expand** the left and right UI in Figma, use the keyboard shortcut: click  in left sidebar of the **File** tab, or use the keyboard shortcut:

- Mac: `⌘ Command` `Shift` `\`
- Windows: `Control` `Shift` `\`

If you select an object, the [navigation bar and left sidebar](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039831974) remains minimized, while the [right sidebar](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039832014) expands to provide access to properties of the selected object. Once all objects are deselected, the right sidebar minimizes again.

![minimize-ui.gif](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/41746989958679)

### Hide or show UI

To hide the entire UI, find **Hide/Show UI** from **Actions**, or use the keyboard shortcut:

- **Mac**: `⌘ Command` `\`
- **Windows**: `Ctrl` `\`
