## 내용

Anyone can report resources or comments that are considered offensive, harassment, or that violate the [Figma Community Guidelines](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360038510573-Figma-Community-Guidelines). Our team will review your report and take action if necessary.

Note: Many disputes and misunderstandings can be resolved directly between Figma users. If one of your creations was posted by someone else, you could first try contacting the violator directly and ask for the content to be taken down. If that doesn’t work, you can [file a copyright report](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=29069632540055).

## Report a resource

1. Open the resource’s Community page.
2. Click **Report resource** on the right-hand side. This will open your computer's default email app and create a new message.
3. Add details of your report in your message. Try to include as much information as possible including user names, descriptions of the content, and links.
4. Click **Send**.

## Report a comment

1. Navigate to the comment you'd like to report.
2. Click in the comment.
3. Click **Report comment** from the options menu.

Note: Comments you report on your own resources will be immediately removed. Comments you report that are made on someone else's resource will be flagged for review and potential removal.
