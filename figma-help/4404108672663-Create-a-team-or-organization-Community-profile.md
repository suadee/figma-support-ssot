## 내용

Who can use this feature

Supported on [any team or plan.](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273)

Only a team admin can manage a team's Community profile

Only an organization admin can manage an organization's Community profile

If you are an admin of a team or organization, you can create a Community profile for that team or organization. This lets you update the Community profile for your team or organization and manage which resources are published to that profile.

**Note:** Organization admins can save Community plugins and widgets for everyone in an organization. This doesn’t require a Community profile. Learn more about [saving plugins and widgets for an organization →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4404239055127-Save-plugins-and-widgets-for-an-organization)

## Create your Community profile

Every Community profile has a unique profile handle, which is used to identify each creator in the Community.

You can use up to 15 alphanumeric characters in your profile handle, including underscores. If the handle is not available, you’ll be prompted to choose another. You can change your profile handle at any time.

Tip: In some cases, you may be able to claim a profile handle that’s in use on an inactive account. To inquire about claiming a handle, contact [Figma Support](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041057214-Get-help-with-Figma#Contact_support).

Some Community actions are only available on specific profile types. This is something to take into account when deciding what kind of profile to create. Learn more about [Community publishing permissions →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041423614-Community-publishing-permissions)

### Create a team Community profile

Claim a profile handle for the team from the team settings:

1. Click the team name in the file browser.
2. Click the  dropdown menu next to the team name and select **View settings**.
3. In the **Community profile** section, select **Set profile handle**.
4. Enter your desired handle in the field provided and click **Create profile** to confirm.

### Create an organization Community profile

Claim a profile handle for the organization from the organization's Admin settings:

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), select **Admin**in the right sidebar.
2. Select the **Settings** tab.
3. Under **Organization profile**, select **Add or change your organization’s public profile handle**.
4. Enter your desired handle in the field provided and click **Create profile** to confirm.

## Customize your profile

After you’ve chosen your profile handle, you can customize your profile and add additional information to introduce your team or organization.

To access your Community profile:

1. Open Figma in the file browser.
2. Select your avatar in the top-left corner to open the account menu.
3. Hover over the Community profile dropdown menu. A list of Community profiles you have access to will display.
4. Select the profile you want to update.

![The Community profile dropdown menu under the account settings menu. The cursor is hovering over the Figma Community profile.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/22402461392023)

You’ll be redirected to the profile’s Community page where you can update the following information:

- **Profile picture:** Your Community profile picture is the same picture you uploaded for your internal Figma profile. You can change this in the [admin settings](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14949911189399-Change-a-team-name-or-icon). Profile picture images must be at least 500 x 500 px.
- **Cover artwork:** Click the  **Pencil** to upload cover artwork.
- **Bio:** Add a brief description of up to 160 characters.
- **Location:** Click to add or update the location of the creator, team, or organization.
- **Websites:** Click to add or update the website. This can be a link to a business website or support page. There are dedicated fields for X (formerly Twitter) and Instagram.

## Manage published resources

Community profiles display all resources published under that profile. This includes external resources that the team or organization is listed as a creator on.

Admins can choose which files appear on the team or organization profile. Only the original publisher of a plugin or widget can manage any team or organization plugins and widgets.

## Delete your Community profile

Delete your profile to remove your public Community profile. Deleting your profile will:

- Unpublish any free files, plugins, or widgets you have published. Paid resources will be [delisted](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/12843697930519-Delist-paid-resources). You can still access these resources within your account.
- Remove your team or organization as a creator from files, plugins, and widgets you contributed to.
- Make your @handle available to other members of the Community.

Note: Deleting your Community profile will not delete your Figma account.

### Delete a team Community profile

1. Click the team name in the file browser.
2. Click the  dropdown menu next to the team name and select **View settings**.
3. In the **Community profile** section, select **Delete profile**.
4. Select **Delete** to confirm.

### Delete an Organization Community profile

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), select **Admin** in the right sidebar.
2. Select the **Settings** tab
3. Under **Organization profile**, select **Delete Community profile**.
4. Select **Delete** to confirm.
