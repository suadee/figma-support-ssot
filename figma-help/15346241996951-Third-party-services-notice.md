## Third party services and organizations

Please be aware that we may obtain information about you from other sources, including from third party services and organizations. If you choose to enable third party services, Figma may access and exchange Service Use Information, Account Information, and Technical Information with the third party on your behalf, in accordance with any permissions granted by you (including your Authorized User(s)). For example, if you access our Services through a third-party service, we may collect information about you from that third party service that you have made available via your privacy settings.
