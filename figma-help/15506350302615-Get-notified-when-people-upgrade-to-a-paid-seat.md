## 내용

In March 2025, Figma introduced updates to how seats and other billing features work. This article describes the updated billing model.

[Learn more about the transition to the updated billing model](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/27468498501527).

Who can use this feature

Available on [all paid plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273)

Admins only

On paid plans, admins use [seat approval settings](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4414038570007) to managing incoming seat requests.

If you use the **Auto-approve seats** setting, you can set up email digest notifications to keep track of seat upgrades.

The email digest notification includes a list of auto-approved new paid seats. Admins choose the cadence of the email digest notification.

Note: You can also receive notifications each time someone requests a seat upgrade.

[Learn more about managing seat requests](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039960434).

## Set up notifications for new paid seats

Professional plan Organization and Enterprise plan

To set up email notifications for upgraded seats:

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin**.
2. Select the **Settings** tab.
3. Under the **Billing** section, click **Auto-approve seat digests**.
4. Select one, or both, from the **Daily** or **Weekly** options:
   - **Daily**: Get an email every day listing new paid seats from the previous day.
   - **Weekly**: Get an email every Monday listing new paid seats from the last Monday to Sunday period.
5. Choose whether to send emails to all admins on the team or only certain admins.
6. Click **Save**.

To set up email notifications for upgraded seats:

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin**.
2. Select the **Settings** tab.
3. Under the **Billing** section, click **Auto-approve seat digests**.
4. Select one, or both, from the **Daily** or **Monthly** options:
   - **Daily**: Get an email every day listing new paid seats from the previous day.
   - **Monthly**: Get an email on the first day of the month listing new paid seats for the previous month.
5. Choose who should get notified:
   - **All admins**: All organization admins
   - **Specific admins**: You can select which organization admins to notify. If you start typing someone’s name and they don’t show up, consider double-checking whether they are an organization admin.
6. Click **Save**.
