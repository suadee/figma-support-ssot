## Common questions

### What is Figma Make?

Figma Make is an AI-driven, prompt-to-app tool that lets you bring ideas and existing Figma designs to life as functional prototypes, web apps, and interactive UI.

You can use the AI chat to start with your designs and content from the Figma Community to create your functional prototypes and web apps.

To learn more, see [Explore Figma Make](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304412302231).

### How do I access Figma Make?

Depending on your plan and seat, there are certain limitations for how you can use Figma Make.

To get started, check out [Create and edit a functional prototype or web app](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304485164695). You can create Figma Make files the same way you [create other new files in Figma](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360038511153-Create-a-new-file). In the file browser, navigate to your drafts. Then, in the upper-right corner, click **Make**.

For organizations and enterprises, Figma Make is only available if AI features are enabled. For Enterprises, admins can toggle AI features on and off for their team or organization.

### Who has access to Figma Make?

Starter plans are a free way to explore Figma Make. If you’re on a Starter plan, there are some limitations to how you can use Figma Make:

- Starter plans cannot use team libraries to [bring style context](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/33024539096471-Bring-style-context-from-a-Figma-Design-library-into-Figma-Make) to Figma Make (team libraries are a paid feature)
- Starter plans can only publish to the public web if they also publish to the Figma Community

Figma Make is currently not available for K-12 [Education](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061214-Figma-for-Education) users. (Figma Make is available for higher education or bootcamp Education users).

For users on paid plans, how you can work with Figma Make depends on your seat. Figma Make is included on the Full seat. Users with a Dev, Collab or View seat can also try Figma Make in drafts.

## Using Figma Make

### Can I edit my functional prototype or web app with Figma Design tools?

While Figma Design tools aren’t directly integrated into Figma Make, you can [use the edit tool](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304485164695#h_01JTEVBJ0527WJSQ40FDX87CRH) to point to specific elements of the preview and edit with by prompting, or directly manipulate properties like font style and text padding.

[](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/33679051823127)

You can also attach your existing designs to your prompts when you’re using the AI chat in Figma Make.

### Why is my attached Figma design not working?

There are a few things to keep in mind when attaching designs from a Figma Design File:

- Use frames and components. Designs that you want to paste or attach to the AI chat in Figma Make must either be a component or added to a frame.
- If you have multiple designs you want to use for the same functional prototype or web app, try attaching only one design to a prompt at a time.

For more information, see our [best practices for attaching designs and images](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304529835671#h_01JTF3AP5ZTV18VCWW048EJKG3).

### Can I paste links to my files into the AI chat?

No. Instead of pasting URLs to your Figma Design files into the prompt box of the AI chat, you should either [attach the design](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304529835671) that you want to the prompt, or copy and paste the frame that contains the design itself into the prompt box.

[](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/33679037660311)

If you try to prompt with a URL, occasionally the model will attempt to interpret the content of the URL itself, not the file it links to. While this sometimes results in the model generating a functional prototype or web app that happens to look similar to your design, generally it results in an unexpected output. Figma Make will not get content from your file via the URL.

### Can I collaborate with others in a Figma Make file?

Yes. Figma Make files that are created by users in Starter plans or users with Full seats on paid plans are multiplayer, allowing users to see when another user is typing or viewing. People with view access can [leave a comment in the file](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/38701587731735).

Figma Make files created by users with Dev, Collab, and View seats cannot be shared without upgrading to a Full seat. Because the files can’t be shared, you cannot collaborate in those files.

### Who can see my content? Can I restrict access?

There are two ways people can access your content:

- **Publishing:** When you publish your functional prototype or web app to the public web, anyone can view it. You can’t restrict access to the published version of the content. For details and questions about publishing, see [Publishing with Figma Make](#h_01JTE4M55FNSTKMQ7P5D2EHFX2).

  **Note:** People who view your published functional prototype or web app don’t have access to the conversation or the Figma Make file that you published from.
- **Sharing:** When you use the share modal to share a Figma Make file, anyone with access to the file can view the conversation and content of the Figma Make file. Figma Make files have the same sharing and permission considerations that apply to other Figma files, such as setting who has `can view` or `can edit` access. For more information, see the [Guide to sharing and permissions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500007609322).

  **Note:** Only users on Starter plans or users with Full seats on paid plans can share Figma Make files and adjust access. Figma Make files created by users with Dev, Collab, and View seats cannot be viewed by other users.

  ![Sharing panel for a Figma Make file showing access permissions and options to invite others or copy a link.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/31860413024279)

### Can I paste my own code into Figma Make and use prompts for designing?

Yes. Figma Make includes a [code editor](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/33649966245783) where you can add your own code. You can then iterate on the code through conversation, including attaching your own designs and images.

### Can I bring my Figma Make file, functional prototype, or web app back to Figma Design?

For users on paid plans, when you're previewing your functional prototype or web app in Figma Make, you can [copy the preview as design layers](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/35060759685015). Then, you can paste those layers into Figma Design and make changes to them. Changes to the layers in Figma Design aren't applied back to the Figma Make file, and the layers aren't automatically tied to your design system.

### Can I change the tone or layout structure of the AI output?

Yes. You can include your preferences in your prompts and the model will adjust accordingly. You can also [use the guidelines.md file](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/33665861260823) that’s available in the code editor.

[](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/33679229366807)

### Can I give microphone and camera access?

In the context of the preview, Figma Make does not currently support this functionality. However, the published version of your web app is able to request microphone and camera access.

Note: You will need to give Figma Make microphone access to [use voice-to-text prompting](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304412302231-Explore-Figma-Make#h_01JTEHMXV40EAX57KXX54CH431).

### What do I do if I get errors I can’t fix or my prompt keeps timing out?

If you need support, see [Explore Figma's help and support resources](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041057214) and our [Support Hub](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/requests/new).

For other issues, see [Troubleshoot in Figma Make](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304610458647).

## Publishing with Figma Make

### What happens when I publish in Figma Make?

**Note:** Only users with Full seats on paid plans and users on Starter plans can publish. Users on Starter plans can only publish to the public web if they also publish to the Figma Community.

When you [publish a functional prototype or web app](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304586129559) that you created in Figma Make, it is made available on the public web with a dedicated URL. Because the functional prototype or web app is available on the public web, it can be accessed by anyone with the URL. By default, your published functional prototype or web app won’t be indexed by search engines, so discoverability will be limited. You can change the discoverability in [the settings for your functional prototype or web app](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304586129559#h_01JTEZTMFFD2MYKY8A5FWH544E).

[You are responsible for content that is published with Figma Make.](#h_01JTE4M55FWHWPBGKYZH29YR4V)

[](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/33679051822231)

### Where is my published web app hosted?

Published functional prototypes or web apps are hosted by Amazon Web Services, while the domain and routing is served by Cloudflare.

### Is the published web app private or public by default?

After publishing your functional prototype or web app, it will be available on the public web. Anyone with the URL can access it. However, by default, the published functional prototype or web app isn't discoverable by search engines, which limits the ways people are able to find it. You can change the discoverability in [the settings for your functional prototype or web app](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304586129559#h_01JTEZTMFFD2MYKY8A5FWH544E).

### Why can’t I publish my Figma Make file?

If the option to publish your Figma Make file is disabled, it’s because your organization has disabled publishing. Contact one of your organization admins to confirm whether publishing is disabled.

![Publishing button disabled with tooltip stating Publishing is disabled by your organization](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/31861879873175)

### Can I track analytics for my published functional prototype or web app?

Figma Make does not include an analytics dashboard. You can [add a Google Analytics ID](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304586129559#h_01JTEZTMFGQCNP55MXYX6Z2B6W) via the settings in a Figma Make file. Other analytics snippets can also be added to the head or body of your published functional prototype or web app in [the settings](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304586129559#h_01JTEZTMFFD2MYKY8A5FWH544E).

### Will I be charged separately for hosting?

No. While publishing is in beta, hosting is included as a part of your existing paid plan.

### Can I use my own custom domain?

Yes, you can use your own [custom domain](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304586129559#h_01JTEZTMFF03FZZM34ZZADEZXB) for your Figma Make creation.

Each plan has a custom domain limit, which is shared between Figma Make and Figma Sites. Organization and Enterprise plans can connect as many custom domains needed, and Professional plans can connect 10 custom domains.

Custom domain limits are subject to change after 2025. While publishing is in beta, custom domains are free of charge through 2025.

### Is SEO supported?

Figma Make optimizes all published files automatically, but does not currently offer specific customizability for SEO optimization.

### Can I delete my Figma Make file? What happens to the data after deletion?

If a functional prototype or web app has been published, deleting the Figma Make file will automatically unpublish it. File data is deleted.

## Content moderation and legal terms

### What are my legal responsibilities when publishing with Figma Make?

Figma is not responsible for content that is published from Figma Make. Published functional prototypes and web apps are accessible to anyone on the public web, and you are responsible for complying with any regulations or laws that apply to your content or any data you collect from visitors to your published Figma Makes.  If you're unsure about what those obligations are or how to comply with them, please consult a lawyer.

Figma reserves the right to remove any published content that is reported and determined to violate Figma’s [Acceptable Use Policy](https://www.figma.com/legal/aup/).

### How do I report a web app or Figma Make file that contains inappropriate or harmful content?

If you are aware of a Figma Make file or content published using Figma Make that contains inappropriate or harmful content, you should submit an abuse report to Figma.

To submit an abuse report:

1. [Submit a request](https://www.figma.com/report-abuse) to Figma.
2. Select the reason for your report, such as **Illegal Content** or **Inappropriate Content**.
3. Provide the URL to the published content or the Figma Make file.
4. If possible, attach screenshots of the content that you are concerned about.

### Can I appeal a takedown?

When your content is taken down due to a violation of Figma’s [Acceptable Use Policy](https://www.figma.com/legal/aup/), you will receive an email informing you of the takedown. To appeal, respond directly to the email that you receive.

While responding to the email is preferred, you can also follow the steps in the previous question to request an appeal.

### How does Figma decide what content violates the guidelines?

Content published with Figma Make is subject to Figma’s [Acceptable Use Policy](https://www.figma.com/legal/aup/).
