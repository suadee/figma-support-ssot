## 내용

Before you Start

Who can use this feature

Available on [all plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273)

Google Chat is a communication platform designed for businesses that enables seamless collaboration and communication among team members. It integrates with other Google Workspace tools, offering real-time messaging, file sharing, and more to enhance productivity and teamwork. Chat provides a solution for group messaging as well as direct messages.

With the Figma add-on for Google Workspace, you can use Google Chat to preview Figma Design and FigJam files, get update notifications, and reply to comments directly from Google Chat. The following table lists what features are supported by product.

|  | Link unfurl | Invites | Comments | Tags | Respond from Google Chat |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Figma Design | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| FigJam | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅\* | ✅ |
| Figma Slides | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Figma Buzz | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Sites | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Make | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |

Currently:

- In FigJam, Google Chat notifications aren't provided for tags in sticky notes. Notifications are otherwise supported.
- Figma Make doesn't have a comment feature, so comments, tags, and respond from Google Chat aren't provided by the integration.

## Get started

1. [Get the Figma add-on for Google Workspace →](https://workspace.google.com/marketplace/app/figma/1035183305161)
2. In the Google Chat navigation panel, under **Apps**, click **Figma**.
3. In the chat, connect your Figma account as prompted.

## Use the Figma app in Google Chat

You can reply to file comments directly in Google Chat. You'll also get notified when:

- You're tagged in a file
- Someone replies to one of your comments
- New comments are made in files you've previously viewed
- You're invited to a new file, project, or team

By default, when you connect Figma and Google Chat, you're automatically enrolled to notifications for invitations, tags, comments, and replies. You can disable notifications by prompting "off" in the Google Chat Figma app.

Previews of Figma files appear in notifications and group chats. Google Chat supports previews for all of Figma's products.

To reply to a comment, click **Reply** in the notification and enter a response. Your reply is added directly to the comment in the Figma file.
