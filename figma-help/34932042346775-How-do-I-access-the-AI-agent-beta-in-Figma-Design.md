## 내용

Who can use this feature

Available on Professional, Organization, and Enterprise plans

Users with Full seats can chat with the agent in Figma Design files

Users with View, Collab, or Dev seats can try the agent in Figma Design files in **Drafts**

Requires edit access

The [agent in Figma Design](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/37998629035799) helps you explore, edit, and refine designs. You can use it to create new designs from scratch, adjust layouts, add or refine content, and get design feedback—all through natural conversation.

## Beta rollout

![](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/40607935416727)

Figma's AI agent is currently in beta and began rolling out to Figma Design users on May 20, 2026.

Over the coming weeks, we're making this feature available to users on the Professional, Organization, and Enterprise plans. You'll know you have access when you receive an email and see a prompt in your Figma Design files pointing to the new  **Agents** item in the sidebar.

### What to expect

- **Gradual rollout**: Access is granted in waves of Figma teams and organizations.
- **Multiple accounts**: If you use Figma with multiple email addresses or accounts, you may receive access at different times for each account. This is normal and expected as part of the gradual rollout.
- **Plans and seat types:** If you have a Full seat on the Professional, Organization, or Enterprise plans, you can use the agent in all your files. Users with a View, Collab, or Dev seat can try it in **Draft** files. The agent is not available on Starter, Government, or Education plans.
- **Language support:** The agent works in multiple languages. Performance is currently best in English, with ongoing improvements planned for other languages.

**Note:** If an admin has deactivated AI features for your Figma team or organization, the agent won't appear in your account during or after the beta. [Learn more about managing AI features](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/17725942479127).

If you’re not sure whether AI features are enabled, [try using another AI feature in Figma Design.](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/23870272542231)

## AI credits during beta

The agent is free to use during the beta. AI credits are not consumed for any prompts or actions. This allows you to explore and experiment without worrying about credit usage.

**Note**: [Figma’s AI credit system](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/33459875669015-How-AI-credits-work#seatcredits) went into effect for Full seat users on March 18, 2026. Once the AI agent becomes generally available, [standard AI credit usage will apply](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/33459875669015-How-AI-credits-work#seatcredits).

## Learn more about the AI agent

While you wait for access, [explore what you can do with Figma's agent](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/37998629035799).

## Frequently asked questions

### When will I get access to Figma's agent?

We're rolling out the agent gradually over the coming weeks. We can't provide specific dates for individual accounts, but all eligible Figma Design users will receive access—as long as AI features are enabled for your account.

To check if AI features are enabled for your account, try using another [AI feature in Figma Design](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/23870272542231).

We’ll automatically send you an email and notify you in your design files when you have access.

### How do I know if I have access? Will I get notified?

![](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/40607891526807)

We’ll notify you by email when you have access to the AI agent.

You’ll also see it in your Figma Design files via the  **Agents** icon in the left sidebar. If you don't see this icon, access hasn't been granted for your account yet.

### Why do I have access on one account but not another?

If you use Figma with multiple email addresses or are part of multiple organizations, you may receive access at different times across your accounts. The rollout is distributed gradually across all users, and different accounts may be included in different rollout waves.

### What does ‘rolling out based on team or organization’ mean?

In Figma, your account is associated with one or more teams or organizations. You can see the name of your team or organization in the sidebar of the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183). If you are part of multiple teams or organization, you can click  to switch between them.

Figma's agent is enabled at the team or organization level, not your account. So some teams or organizations you’re part of may get access before others.

### What can I do with the agent? What’s the difference between this feature and Figma Make?

[Figma's agent](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/37998629035799) helps you with design work through natural conversation. You can:

**Accelerate divergent thinking to push designs farther**

- Generate, remix, and refine designs while incorporating your design systems
- Explore styles and layouts to get inspired

**Stay in the flow by spending less time on busywork**

- Automate bulk edits
- Update and maintain design systems
- Update designs follow design systems best practices
- Fill designs with realistic content in bulk

**Summarize existing feedback and get new feedback for your designs**

- Brainstorm improvements and edge cases
- Ask for feedback from different personas, like a VP of product or new users
- Summarize comments and take action

From there, you can use Figma Make to turn your designs into interactive prototypes and publish them to the web. [Explore Figma Make.](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304412302231)

### Will I be charged AI credits during the beta?

The agent is **free during the beta period**. AI credits won't be consumed for any prompts or actions.

Figma’s AI features use a credit system. To ensure everyone can try and use these features, AI credits are included with every seat on every plan. When the agent is generally available, [Figma’s AI credit system](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/33459875669015) will apply.

### Do I need any special plan or subscription?

The agent is available to the following Figma Design users:

- Full seats can use the agent in all Figma Design files
- View, Dev, and Collab seats can try the agent in **Draft** files
- The agent is included on the Professional, Organization, or Enterprise plans with [AI features enabled](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/17725942479127)

### What if I'm experiencing issues or have feedback?

If you encounter issues or have suggestions, here are a few tips that can help:

1. Refresh the page and try the request again.
2. Try creating a new thread using the  button in the top right corner of the chat.
3. Report bugs or issues through [Figma's standard support channels](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041057214-Explore-Figma-s-help-and-support-resources). Click  on any message from the agent to copy the debug details. Including these details in your message helps our team understand the issue.

![](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/40557705659287)

**Tip**: You can also use the feedback option in the chat to give a thumbs up or thumbs down on the output.

### When will the agent be generally available to everyone?

We don't have a confirmed date for general availability yet. The agent will remain in beta as we gather feedback, improve the feature, and ensure it meets quality standards.

### What happens to my beta access when the agent becomes generally available?

Your access will continue when the beta ends and the agent becomes generally available. At that point, using the agent will count towards your [monthly AI credit balance](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/33459875669015).

### How is my data used by the agent?

Learn more about Figma’s approach to building AI features—including data privacy, security and model training—via [Building Figma AI](https://www.figma.com/ai/our-approach/).

### Can I disable AI tools for my organization?

Yes, Figma admins can disable AI tools for everyone in their team or organization. [Learn more about managing AI feature access and settings as an admin](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/17725942479127).

### Can I opt out of the agent but keep other Figma AI tools?

It’s not currently possible to opt out of individual Figma AI tools.

### Are there limits to how much I can use this feature while it's in beta?

There are monthly limits for this feature, which helps to manage capacity and costs while it's free in beta. You'll see an in-product message if you hit the monthly limit. The limit resets each month and won't affect your credit limits for other AI features.
