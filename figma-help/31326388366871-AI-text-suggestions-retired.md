## 내용

**AI text suggestions have been retired from Figma Design.**  
AI text suggestions launched at Config 2025 and are no longer available.

**What were AI text suggestions?**  
AI text suggestions automatically completed text strings based on the context of your design. They appeared as transparent text while you typed when relevant suggestions were available. ![Two designs showing a before and after accepting AI text suggestions. The first shows transparent suggestions in a text field; the second shows that "tab" will accept suggestions and place the text in the field in a darker text.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/31803793530903)

To see which AI tools are available, check out [AI tools in Figma Design](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/23870272542231-Use-AI-tools-in-Figma-Design).
