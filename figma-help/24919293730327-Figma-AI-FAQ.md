## 내용

Figma AI is designed to help you work smarter and bring your ideas to life faster. AI tools can be found throughout Figma's products.

[Learn more about Figma AI](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24039793359767).

## Frequently asked questions

### **What AI features are available?**

For a full list of available AI features and which products they are available in, see [About Figma AI](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24039793359767).

To see a list of available AI features by product, check out the following resources:

- [Use AI tools in Figma Design](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/23870272542231)
- [Use AI tools in FigJam](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/16822138920343)
- [Use AI tools in Figma Slides](https://help.figma.com/hc/articles/31433930664215)

- [Use AI tools in Figma Buzz](https://help.figma.com/hc/articles/31434032783383)

### **Why don’t I have access to AI features?**

If you’re not seeing AI features, it may be due to one of the following reasons:

- **You're on the Starter plan**: Most Figma AI features are only available on paid plans. Starter plans do not include Figma AI, except for [code layers in Figma Sites](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31242824165143-Guide-to-code-layers-in-Figma-Sites) or [limited access to Figma Make](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31722591905559-Figma-Make-FAQs#h_01JZQX7SQ7RX4SJ65DHDTYS2EK).
- **Your team or organization is opted out:** Your admin has already chosen to opt your team or organization out of AI features. Please contact your admin if you have questions.
- **You're on a Figma for Education plan:** AI feature availability depends on your Education account type.
  - AI tools are available for higher education and bootcamp Education users. AI tools are not available for K-12 Education users.
  - Some AI features may have limited availability for Education users. For example, [visual search](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24037716110615-Find-assets-and-designs-using-AI) in the Figma editor is not available, but visual search for Community resources is available.
  - Figma Make is available for higher education and bootcamp Education users. It is not available to K-12 users.
  - If you have any questions, please [submit a request](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/requests/new).
- **You're using Figma for Government:** Figma Make is available to organizations using [Figma for Government](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/22932461365015), but other Figma AI features are not currently available.

### **How do I access Figma AI features?**

**In Figma Design, FigJam, Figma Slides, and Figma Sites:**

You can access Figma AI tools using  **Actions** in the toolbar.

**In Figma Buzz:**

You can access Figma AI tools by selecting  **AI tools** from the inline toolbar.

### **Will enabling AI features include access to AI features in all products?**

Yes. Enabling AI features grants access to AI tools across Figma Design, FigJam, Figma Slides, Figma Buzz, and Figma Sites. It also includes Figma Make.

While admins manage the overall availability of AI across teams and organizations, some AI features are limited to certain plans or by a user’s permissions.

[Learn more about managing AI settings.](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/17725942479127)

### **Is usage limited?**

For information about AI credits and usage, see [How AI credits work](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/33459875669015).

### **Are AI features available in drafts?**

AI features are available in drafts for all users.
