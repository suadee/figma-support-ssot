## 내용

Before you start

Who can use this feature

Available on the [Organization and Enterprise plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273)

Any member of an organization can publish a private plugin to the organization

Only organization admins can publish public plugins to an organization profile. [Publish plugins to the Community →](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360042293394)

Organizations often have internal tools and systems, with private information or data, that can't be shared outside the organization.

Private plugins allows members of the organization to use organization-specific plugins, without having to share them with the rest of the Community.

- Any member of an organization can make and share a private plugin
- Only team admins can publish plugins to the team's Community profile
- Only organization admins can share plugins to the organization's Community profile

## Publish private plugins

When [publishing a plugin](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360042293394), you can use the **Publish to** setting to choose whether to publish the plugin to the organization or the Community. Private plugins don't need to go through the review process before they are available to use.

![](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/34104162522263)

To create a private plugin for an organization, follow the steps to [publish plugins to the Figma Community](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360042293394). On **Add the final details** page, set **Publish to** to your organization.

Only the plugin's original publisher can change the plugin's access. They can update this setting by publishing an update to the plugin.

Update the **Publish to** setting to change who can access the plugin. If you're changing a plugin from private to public, it will need to go through Figma's plugin and widget review process.

[Learn how to publish](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360042293394) [plugin](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360042293394)[s](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360042293394) [→](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360042293394)

If the original publisher has left the organization and you need to transfer ownership of a private plugin, [contact the Figma Support team](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041057214).

## View private plugins

To find and install plugins that are private to your organization:

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click **All teams** or **All workspaces**.
2. Select the **Plugins** header at the top of the page.
3. Browse through any plugins that are private to your organization.
4. Click the **↓ Save** button to add the plugin to your account.
