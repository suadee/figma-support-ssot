## 내용

Who can use this feature

Supported on [any team or plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273)

Only the current owner of a team can transfer ownership to another team member.

The person who originally created the team is listed as the owner. If you are leaving the team, or want to hand that responsibility to someone else, you can transfer ownership of the team.

Only the current owner can transfer the ownership of the team to another member. They can transfer ownership to any existing team member, including a member with a free seat or `can view` access to the team.

Caution: This process only changes the ownership of the team. On the Professional plan, it won't update the billing details for the team's subscription. If you also pay for the subscription, you will need to [update the billing details for your team](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034), before you transfer ownership.

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), navigate to the team where you'd like to transfer ownership.
   - On the Starter or Professional plan, select  **All projects** from the left sidebar.
   - On the Organization or Enterprise plan without workspaces enabled, select  **All teams** from the left sidebar. Then, select a team.
   - On the Enterprise plan with workspaces enabled, select  **All workspaces** from the left sidebar. Then, select a team from the **Your teams** tab.
2. On the team page, click the  **down arrow** next to the team name and select **View members**.
3. Click the relevant person's existing permissions dropdown and change it to **Owner**.
4. Figma will ask you to confirm the ownership transfer. It's not possible to undo this action, but the new owner can transfer ownership again. Click **Transfer ownership** to confirm.
5. Figma will make that member the owner and give you an admin role on the team instead.

**Note:** Figma won't remove you from the team when you transfer ownership. You will stay on the team as a team admin. If you'd like, you can [leave the team](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039965653), or another admin can remove you.

**Our team owner has left the company. How can we transfer the ownership to someone else?**

If the original team owner has left the company, there are a couple of ways you can update ownership:

- **Option 1: Recover the original owner’s account**
  - If your IT team can forward emails from the original owner’s email address, you may be able to reset their password and log in. Once signed in, you can transfer ownership to your own account or another team member, and then leave the team.
- **Option 2: Create a new team**
  - If no one has access to the original owner’s account, an admin on the team can cancel the current subscription and allow it to expire.
  - If there are no admins left, contact Figma support with the last 4 digits and expiration date of the credit card used, along with the date and amount of the most recent payment. Our team can remove the card on file to prevent renewals.
  - After the subscription is canceled, you can [create a new team](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039484194-Create-and-explore-a-team), [upgrade it to a paid plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360046216313-Upgrade-or-downgrade-your-plan), and [move the original team’s projects into the new team](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/12729516837271-Move-a-project).
