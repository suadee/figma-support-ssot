## 내용

Who can use this feature

Supported on the [Organization and Enterprise plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273).

Only organization admins can enable or disable audio.

Organization admins can turn off [the Audio feature](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500004414622) for the entire organization. This will prevent members and guests from using Audio in any Figma Design or FigJam files in the organization.

1. Open the organization in Figma.
2. Select **Admin** from the sidebar.
3. Select the **Settings** tab.
4. In the **Other** section, toggle **Audio** to the  off position.
