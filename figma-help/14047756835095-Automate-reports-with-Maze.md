## 내용

Maze empowers product teams to continuously collect and consume user insights across the entire development cycle. With solutions for participant recruitment, product research and reporting, Maze helps teams build the habit of continuous product discovery.

Maze allows you to test your Figma prototype with real users. Once you start receiving responses on your tests, you’ll be able to embed your automated reports directly in FigJam.

[Try out the Maze integration with this FigJam template →](https://www.figma.com/community/file/1231216682592148721/Maze-FigJam-Embed)

Use Maze to:

- Run usability tests on prototypes and websites
- Get quantitative data such as success rates, misclick rates and duration
- Review click heat maps for each screen
- Ask closed and open-ended follow-up questions
- Generate and share reports with key analytics and insights

To get started, follow the [Maze Quick Start guide](https://help.maze.co/hc/en-us/articles/360052722793-Maze-quick-start) and [sign up for a Maze account](https://app.maze.co/signup) →

# Copy link from MazeSharing a report by clicking "Share report" button and copying the public link from the options available in the panel.

1. Open the [report page](https://help.maze.co/hc/en-us/articles/360052722693-Your-maze-reports) of your live maze.
2. Click **Share report**in the top-right of the navigation bar.
3. Check the box to **Copy embed URL**.
4. Click **Copy** next to the generated link.

[Learn more about using Maze reports ->](https://help.maze.co/hc/en-us/articles/360052722693-Your-maze-reports)

# Embed Maze in FigJamAn animation showing someone embedding a Maze report link onto a FigJam canvas, demonstrating integration and real-time syncing of user insights.

1. Open a new or existing FigJam file.
2. Paste the link on the board where you want to embed your Maze report.

**Note:** Reports are automatically synced. The latest results from Maze will be immediately visible in FigJam.
