Figma Buzz was released in [open beta](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4406787442711) at Config 2025. It is currently available on all plans and seat types. [Learn more about what’s included in the beta.](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4406787442711)

While in beta, Figma Buzz is available on all seats and plans.

Video import and export is available on paid plans.

Using videos in your assets opens up new possibilities—whether you're building engaging social media stories, showcasing product demos, or creating dynamic marketing content. Instead of relying on static visuals, you can now integrate motion, sound, and storytelling directly into your Figma Buzz projects.

## Import videos

You can import .mp4 and .mov files to your Figma Buzz file.

Videos can be added either directly to an asset, or via the **Edit content** tab when working with templates that support media customization.

### Add videos directly to asset

1. Select  **Media** from the left navigation bar.
2. Click **Upload**.
3. Choose one or more video files from your device.
4. Click to place the video on the asset.

You can also drag and drop files directly from your device to your Figma Buzz file.

### Add videos to template

If you’re [using a template](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31271689852823-Find-and-use-Figma-Buzz-templates), you can replace placeholder media with a video via the **Edit content** tab:

1. [Add a template](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31271689852823-Find-and-use-Figma-Buzz-templates) to your file.
2. Select  **Edit content** from the left navigation bar.
3. In the **Media** section, hover over an existing image or video and click **Choose media**.
4. Select a file from your device to upload.

**Note**: If you don’t see any editable media fields in the **Edit content** tab, the template creator may have locked those assets.

[Learn more about using templates with guidelines in Figma Buzz](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31271733056023-Use-templates-with-guidelines-in-Figma-Buzz).

## Adjust videos

Once you’ve added a video to your asset, you can playback the video you’ve added by selecting the video on the asset and clicking the  **Play video** button.

### Video quality

You can enhance the visual quality of your video using the **Adjust video** tool:

1. Select the video.
2. From the inline toolbar, open the **Fill** dropdown.
3. Select the **Image & video** tab.
4. Click  **Adjust video**.
5. Use the available sliders to modify:

- Exposure
- Contrast
- Saturation
- Temperature
- Tint
- Highlights
- Shadows

![Adjust video quality in Figma Buzz.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/33799666552855)

### Volume

To control volume settings:

- Open the  **Volume** dropdown from the inline toolbar.
- Choose whether to mute or include audio during playback or export.

### Trim videos

Shorten or cut your video directly in your file.

1. Select your video on the asset.
2. From the inline toolbar, click  **Trim.**
3. Use the handles in the timeline to set your start and end points.
4. Click  the checkmark to confirm your edits.

## Export videos

Once your assets are ready, you can export them just like static images—except you’ll have additional controls for video output.

[Learn more about exporting assets from Figma Buzz](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31271832936343-Export-assets-from-Figma-Buzz).
