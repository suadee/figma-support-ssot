## 내용

Before you start

Who can use this feature

Available on the [Enterprise plan.](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273-Choose-a-Figma-Plan)

Organization admins only

Restricting who can create teams gives organization admins greater control over team creation and organizational growth.

There are two settings for managing team creation:

1. **Anyone:** Any member of this organization can create teams
2. **Admins only:** Only organization and workspace admins can create teams

When [team creation](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039484194) is limited to admins only, the  **Team** button will be grayed out for members without admin privileges.

## Update team creation setting

To change who can create a team in an organization:

1. Open the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183-Guide-to-the-file-browser) by logging in to your account at [figma.com](https://figma.com/).
   - If you’re in a specific Figma file, click the  Figma menu > **Back to files.**
2. Click  **Admin.**
3. Select the  **Settings** tab.
4. In the **Team management** section, select **Creating teams.**
5. Choose a setting:
   1. **Anyone**: Any member of the organization can create teams
   2. **Admins only**: Only organization and workspace admins can create teams
6. Click **Save.**
