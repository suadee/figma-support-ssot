## 내용

Don't start from scratch—jumpstart your designs with templates and tools from your team, organization or the Figma Community. Whether you’re looking for inspiration or customizable assets, you can find them easily right from the file browser.

## Find resources from your organization

Access your team or organization’s internal resources under **Resources**. Each team or organization you’re a part of has its own Resource space. [Learn how to switch your team or organization](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500005165741).

![Resources panel in Figma file browser showing team-curated templates, plugins, and widgets with previews of recent additions.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/34813516461591)

Your [paid plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273-Figma-plans-and-features) determines the types of internal templates and tools that are shown:

|  |  |  |  |  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Plan** | **Figma Make** | **Figma Buzz** | **FigJam** | **Figma Slides** | **Plugins** | **Widgets** |
| Professional | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | X | X |
| Organization | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Enterprise | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |

Select a template category to view only those types of resources, organized by team. Click a template to open it in a corresponding file type, or click a plugin or widget and choose a file to open.

## Resources FAQ

**What are the differences in access to internal resources for Professional teams, Organizations, and Enterprise users?**

- Professional teams have access to libraries.
- Organization and Enterprise users have access to private plugins and widgets, while Pro users do not.

**What will happen to the Resources categories if I switch my team or organization in the dropdown?**

You will see different categories and internal brand resources based on the team/org you have selected.

**How do I find templates and resources from the Figma Community?**

You can find resources shared publicly in the Figma Community by selecting the Community tab in the file browser. You can also navigate to [figma.com/community](https://www.figma.com/community) for full Community features such as commenting and saving.

**Can you publish a template to a team within an org? Does it vary by resource type?**

- You can publish a template to your current team or the entire organization.
- Internal libraries are published at the team level.

**Why can't I see anything within Resources?**

Resources shared across a team are only available for people on paid plans. If you’re on the Starter plan, you can find and use templates or tools from the Figma Community. [Learn more about the Figma Community.](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360038510693)
