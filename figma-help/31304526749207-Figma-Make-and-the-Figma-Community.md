## 내용

Before you start

Who can use this feature

Figma Make is available for [Full seats](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039960434) on paid plans.

You can try Figma Make on [other seats and plans](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31722591905559-Figma-Make-FAQs#h_01JZQX7SQ7RX4SJ65DHDTYS2EK).

Figma Make includes the ability to [attach designs from the Figma Community](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304529835671#h_01JTF3AP5Z29GYYWW83WV92Y3V) directly to prompts in Figma Make.

## Attribution

When a Community design is attached to a prompt in Figma Make, a reminder appears above the prompt box that lists the designs. Additionally, Figma Make creates an `Attributions.md` file that includes a list of all assets that require attribution. The file is packaged with the functional prototype or web app to ensure proper attribution to original creators.

## For Figma Make users

When you’re attaching designs to your Figma Make file, you can search the Community for content to attach to your prompts. To learn how to attach designs, including community content, see [Attach designs and images to a prompt](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304529835671).

![](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/31865245617687)

### Paid resources

If you’ve previously paid for a design file, you can use the content from the file by copying and pasting frames and components into the prompt box of the AI chat. Paid resources won’t appear in the Community search.

## For Community file creators

It’s important to Figma that we do our best to ensure the files you create and share in the Community, including the designs and components, [get proper attribution](#h_01JTF3M75X3SMKE10YKSTGF3W9) when they’re being used in Figma Make.

We understand that you may want to opt your Community files out of appearing in the search. If you don’t want your Community files to appear in the search, you can opt your entire account out by [submitting a request](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/requests/new) to Figma. You cannot opt out individual files.
