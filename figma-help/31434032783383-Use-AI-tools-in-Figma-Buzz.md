## 내용

Before you start

Who can use this feature

While in beta, Figma Buzz and its AI tools are available to all seat types.

AI outputs may be misleading or wrong. The information provided by the tools should be regarded as a general reference and not a fact or a substitute for expert advice or your own research.

[Learn more about accuracy and responsibilities when using Figma AI](#considerations).

AI tools let you leverage the knowledge of large language models to help speed up your work in Figma Buzz. In this article, you can explore all the AI features available in Figma Buzz.

**Tip**: You can also use AI tools in other Figma products.

[Learn more about Figma AI and see a breakdown of AI features available by product](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24039793359767).

## Available AI tools in Figma Buzz

### Rewrite, translate, or shorten text

Rewrite text to give it a different tone, translate it to another language, or shorten it to fit your designs.

[Learn more about updating text with AI.](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24004868368919)

### Make and edit images

Generate and edit unique images using a written prompt.

[Learn more about making an image with AI.](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24004542669463)

### Remove background

Remove an image’s background to isolate the main subject.

[Learn more about removing an image background with AI.](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24004542669463)

### Boost resolution

Improve sharpness and clarity of low-resolution images.

[Learn more about boosting resolution with AI.](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24004542669463)

### Expand image

Extend an image beyond its original borders.

[Learn more about making and editing images with AI.](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24004542669463-Make-or-edit-an-image-with-AI)

### Isolate object

Select and separate an object within an image so you can move, resize, or reuse it in other assets.

[Learn more about making and editing images with AI](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24004542669463-Make-or-edit-an-image-with-AI).

### Erase object

Remove unwanted objects or text from an image.

[Learn more about making and editing images with AI](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24004542669463-Make-or-edit-an-image-with-AI).

### Vectorize image

Convert a static image into editable vectors so you can refine visuals more easily.

[Learn more about vectorizing images](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/38031452710807-Convert-static-images-to-vector-layers).

## AI considerations and best practices

There are a few things to keep in mind when using any AI tool:

### Accuracy

AI-powered tools are designed to provide information and results based on artificial intelligence algorithms and data collected from around the internet. It’s important to note that the accuracy and reliability of these results may vary.

There is a possibility of inaccuracies, omissions, or misleading outcomes due to the complexities and limitations of AI technologies. Therefore, the information provided by the tool should be regarded as a general reference and not a fact or a substitute for expert advice or your own research.

### Your responsibilities

- You should exercise your own judgment and discretion when using AI-powered tools. It’s essential to verify and cross-check the information provided by the tool before making any decisions, taking actions, or relying on it for critical matters.
- You should let others know when you’re using or presenting AI-generated outputs.
- You will only use AI features in accordance with [Figma’s Acceptable Use Policy.](https://www.figma.com/legal/aup/)
