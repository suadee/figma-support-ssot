## 내용

**Important:** This article covers how to manage plugin subscriptions purchased from the [Figma Community](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360038510693-Guide-to-the-Figma-Community). For information on upgrading or downgrading your **Figma plan**, see [this article](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360046216313-Upgrade-or-downgrade-your-plan) instead.

If you purchased a Community plugin subscription using Figma’s payment platform, you can manage your subscriptions from your Stripe customer portal:

- Cancel or renew your plugin subscriptions
- Update your payment method
- Change your billing information
- Review your purchase history

Note: If you subscribed to a plugin using a third-party payment site, you’ll need to contact the creator directly to manage your subscription.

To access your Stripe customer portal:

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183-Guide-to-the-file-browser), select your avatar in the top-right corner to open the account menu.
2. Click **Settings**.
3. Select the **Community** tab.
4. Under Community purchases, locate the subscription you want to manage.
5. From the dropdown, select **Manage subscription**.

You will be redirected to your Stripe customer portal where you can cancel or renew your plugin subscriptions or update your payment method.

![Dropdown menu in the Community tab showing options to manage, contact creator, or get support for plugin subscriptions.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/29316563126167)

Note: Plugin subscriptions are not refunded when canceled. You can cancel a plugin subscription to prevent it from being automatically renewed. You will still be able to access the plugin until the end of your current billing cycle.
