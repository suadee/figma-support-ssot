## 내용

Generally, for Starter, Pro, and Organization plans, any non-guest member of a team, workspace, or organization is able to view the admins. Guests on any plan cannot view admins.

### Teams

To view the admins for a team, [go to a team](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039957674-View-and-join-teams#h_01K6NPZSQ7FS09XNMVEYH0NPXH) in the file browser. Then, do one of the following:

- Click  next to the team name, and then **View Members**.
- Or, in the upper-right corner, click **Share**.

The **Invite other members** modal appears, displaying a list of team members. The owner and admins of the team are identified in the list of members.

To view the admins of a hidden team, you must be a member of that team.

Guests aren’t able to view team members, including the team owner and admins.

![](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/38513349601943)

### Workspaces

To view the admins for a workspace, [go to a workspace](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/7576392133527-Guide-to-workspaces#h_01HFSQ607HCJQHSG1G4WK39RAT) in the file browser. The list of members appears on the right side of the file browser. You can also click  in the upper-right corner to open the **Members** modal, and then click **Admins**.

![](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/38513331256471)

### Organizations

To view the admins for an organization, [go to an organization](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183-Guide-to-the-file-browser#h_01HFSGPGYPQW6XZQZ0H4NT8M7B) in the file browser. If you're in your drafts, click **All teams** or **All workspaces**. The list of admins appears on the right side of the file browser.

You must be a [member](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557314967-Members-versus-guests#members) of the organization to view the admins. Organization [guests](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557314967-Members-versus-guests#guests) cannot view admins.
