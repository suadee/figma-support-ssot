## About this collection

**Get hands-on practice**  
Grab a copy of the [companion file](https://www.figma.com/community/file/1627053215388532830) to follow along with hands-on examples and practice projects from this collection.

Welcome to the Components collection! This comprehensive learning guide will help you become more adept at creating and using components in Figma Design. Whether you’re new to the world of components, or are looking to level up your design system skills, we’ll guide you through essential concepts with clear explanations and interactive projects that give you hands-on practice directly in design files.

When you complete the collection, you’ll have a solid understanding of the following concepts and be ready to implement them in your own projects:

- [**Components and instances**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/39635555294743-Components-collection-Components-fundamentals)**:** The fundamental building blocks of scalable design systems
- [**Nested instances**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/39723486706455-Components-collection-Nesting-instances-fundamentals)**:** Create complex, flexible components by combining simpler ones
- [**Component properties**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/39636407507735-Components-collection-Component-property-fundamentals)**:** Make adaptable components with customizable controls
- [**Variants**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/39636737843735-Components-collection-Variants-and-component-set-fundamentals)**:** Organize component variations efficiently within component sets
- [**Slots**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/39745565646871-Components-collection-Slots-fundamentals)**:** Create flexible content areas within components for dynamic layouts
- [**Interactive components**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/39747190144023-Components-collection-Interactive-components-fundamentals)**:** Build realistic prototyping behavior directly into your components
- [**Libraries**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/39723547036055-Components-collection-Library-fundamentals)**:** Share reusable design assets with your entire team
- [**Component management**](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/39747637290263-Components-collection-Tips-for-component-management)**:** Learn best practices for organizing, documenting, and managing your component library
