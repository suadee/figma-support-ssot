## 내용

AI is transforming how teams design, build, and collaborate. With Figma's AI products and features, customers can move faster and unlock new creative potential.

As with any new technology, it also raises important questions—especially around intellectual property. Some customers have asked about the risk of copyright claims related to AI-generated content.

To help address this, we’re able to expand our indemnity commitment to Organization and Enterprise customers to provide a layer of copyright protection for outputs generated from Figma AI.

This article explains what AI copyright protection Figma offers and how to request it.

## Who can add AI copyright protection?

AI copyright protection is available to all customers on Organization and Enterprise plans at no additional cost.

## How do I add AI copyright protection?

Eligible customers may secure AI copyright protection by reaching out to their dedicated sales representative. If you don’t have a dedicated sales representative, reach out to our [Support team](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=24342800450071).

## Need help?

If you’re not sure whether you’re eligible or have questions about the process, reach out to our [Support team](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=24342800450071).
