## 내용

Who can use this feature

Available on the [Enterprise plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273)

Only [organization admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439) can add or remove workspace admins

[Workspaces](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4409676189207) are groups of teams, people, and resources within an organization. They add another layer of structure within organizations.

[Workspace admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439-Admins-in-Figma#workspace-admin) have a level of access below an organization admin. They can only manage the workspaces you assign them to and teams within the workspaces that aren't [hidden](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039970673-Team-permissions#h_01K6NBT5RPHEE8JD14YY8JD0YS). Workspace admins can:

- [Assign teams to workspaces](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4419471354007)
- [Assign people to workspaces](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4419365500695)
- [Set default teams for the workspace](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4419450594455)
- [Set default libraries and modes for the workspace](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/21310245473815)
- [Approve libraries for a workspace](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/21310245473815)
- [Remove people from the organization](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040453453)
- Act as a [team admin](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439-Admins-in-Figma#team-admin) for teams in the workspace

## Manage workspace admins

Organization admins can assign workspaces admins from **Admin > Workspaces**.

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), select **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select the **Content** tab.
3. Click **Workspaces**.
4. Click  next to the workspace.
5. Select **Edit workspace** to make changes to the group.

### Add workspace admin

1. Select the **Workspace admin(s)** field .
2. Start typing in a member's name.
3. Press `Enter` or click to add them as a workspace admin.
4. Click **Save changes** to apply

### Remove workspace admin

1. Click  next to an admin to remove them.
2. Click **Save changes** to apply
