## 내용

Who can use this feature

Available on the [Enterprise plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328273)

[Organization admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439) and [workspace admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439)

[Workspaces](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4409676189207) are groups of teams, people, and resources within an organization. They add another layer of structure within organizations. You can group or structure related teams by assigning them to a workspace.

- [Organization admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439) can assign, reassign, or unassign any team in the organization.
- [Workspace admins](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557724439) can claim unassigned teams, move teams between workspaces where they’re an admin, and unassign teams from their workspaces.

## Assign teams to a workspace

Workspace admin Organization admin

Assign teams to any workspace where you’re an admin.

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select the relevant workspace in the sidebar.
3. Select the **Teams** tab and click **+ Assign teams**.
4. Navigate to, or search for, teams you'd like to assign and mark the checkbox next to their name.
5. Click **Assign teams**.

**Note:** Workspace admins can also [set default teams for a workspace](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4419450594455). Figma adds members to these default teams when they are assigned to the workspace.

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **Content** at the top of the page, then switch to the **Teams** tab.
3. Navigate to the team you'd like to assign.
4. In the **Workspace** column, click **Unassigned** and select a new workspace.

**Tip**: To assign teams in bulk, hover over a team and click the checkbox at the start of the row. Select multiple teams, then click **Change workspace** from the menu that appears at the bottom of the screen.

**Note**: Assigning a team to a [hidden workspace](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/19375017901335) won't change team members' access to the team. However, they will only be able to view details about the workspace if they are specifically assigned to it.

## Move teams between workspaces

Workspace admin Organization admin

Workspace admins can only move teams between workspaces they manage.

1. From the file browser, select  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select the relevant workspace in the sidebar.
3. Select the **Teams** tab to view teams in the current workspace.
4. Navigate to, or search for, the team you'd like to move.
5. In the **Workspace** column for that team, click the current workspace name and select a new one. If you don't see the workspace where you'd like to move the team, double check if you're a workspace admin of that workspace.

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **Content** at the top of the page, then switch to the **Teams** tab.
3. In the **Workspace** column, click the name of the current workspace the team is assigned to and select a new one.

**Tip**: To assign teams in bulk, hover over a team and click the checkbox at the start of the row. Select multiple teams, then click **Change workspace** from the menu that appears at the bottom of the screen.

## Unassign a team from a workspace

Unassign teams from the current workspace. This won’t affect any files and projects in the team.

If a team is removed from a workspace and its permissions were set to **Anyone in [workspace]** can access, those permissions will change to **Only invited users**. [Learn more about team permissions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039970673-Team-permissions).

Workspace admin Organization admin

Unassign teams from the current workspace. This won’t remove anyone’s access to the team, or affect any files and projects in the team.

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click  **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Select the relevant workspace in the sidebar.
3. Select the **Teams** tab.
4. Navigate to, or search for, the team you'd like to unassign.
5. In the **Workspace** column, click the current workspace name and select **Unassign**.

1. From the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183), click **Admin** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **Content** at the top of the page, then switch to the **Teams** tab.
3. In the **Workspace** column, click the current workspace name and select **Unassign**.

**Tip**: To assign teams in bulk, hover over a team and click the checkbox at the start of the row. Select multiple teams, then click **Change workspace** from the menu that appears at the bottom of the screen.
