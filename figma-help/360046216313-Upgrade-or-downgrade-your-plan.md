## 내용

Before you start

Who can use this feature

Anyone with `can edit` permissions on a team can upgrade a Starter team

Team admins or owners can upgrade or downgrade a Professional team

Organization admins can cancel an Organization plan

Organization admins can upgrade organizations by [contacting our sales team](https://www.figma.com/contact/).

With a paid plan, you gain access to enhanced design and collaboration features, improved administration tools, and advanced security capabilities to support the growth of Figma within your organization.

Tip: Visit [Figma’s pricing page](https://www.figma.com/pricing/) to learn more about each plan.

To learn more about billing, check out the [Guide to billing at Figma](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/29717597009431).

## Upgrade your plan

**Upgrade to the Professional plan**

When you upgrade a team to the Professional plan, your subscription cost
is calculated based on the number of seats in your plan. You can choose
how
many seats to purchase at the time of upgrade, and adjust seat counts
during
each renewal period or while you're still subscribed.

[Learn more about managing billing on the Professional plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041061034).

1. Open the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183-Guide-to-the-file-browser) by
   logging in to your account at [figma.com](https://figma.com/).
   - If you’re in a specific Figma file, you can access the file
     browser
     by clicking **Main menu** >
     **Back to files** from the left sidebar toolbar.
2. Next to the team name on the left sidebar, click the
   **Free**
   badge.
3. In the popup modal, select the Professional plan.
4. On the next page, select seat types for any existing team members.
   You
   can also add additional seats for any future team members.
5. Click **Next: Payment**.
6. Enter in your payment information.
7. Click **Next: Review**.
8. Review your plan and subscription details.
9. After agreeing to Figma’s terms of services, click
   **Complete purchase**.

![To upgrade a Starter team, click the Free badge next to the team name in the file browser.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/30713035600791)

Figma will charge your credit card and update your account immediately.

**Note**: We only support payment by credit card
or
SEPA debit on the Professional plan. Payment via Paypal, ACH
or wire
transfer isn’t currently available.

**Upgrade to the Organization plan**

You can upgrade to the Organization plan by yourself, or by getting
in touch with our sales team.

[Learn more about billing on the Organization plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328293).

Take a look at the table below to see which approach we recommend for
your
company.

|  | Upgrade yourself | Upgrade with Figma sales team |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Migrate teams you are an admin of into a Figma organization | ✓ | ✓ |
| Migrate all teams registered with your company domain into a Figma organization using domain capture | After upgrade | ✓ |

Depending on which approach is right for you, you can
[get in touch with our sales team](https://www.figma.com/contact/)
or follow the instructions below to upgrade on your own.

Here are some things you should note before you begin:

- You’ll need a Figma account with a work email address to upgrade
  to the
  Organization plan. For example:
  `[yourname]@examplecompany.com`
  .
- During the upgrade, you can migrate any teams you’re an admin of
  into
  the new Figma organization. If you’re an admin of a team that isn’t
  listed
  as an option to migrate, double check whether your account in that
  team
  is an admin and uses the same work email address.
- If any teams included in the upgrade are subscribed to the Professional
  plan, Figma will automatically generate a credit for the remainder
  of
  the subscription(s). This applies to both monthly or annual subscriptions.
  The credit amount will be applied to the first invoice created after
  upgrading to the Organization plan.
- People with email addresses from other companies will be converted
  into
  guests in your new organization. These guests will retain their existing
  level of permissions in files, projects, and teams they access.
- When you upgrade, Figma will move your existing teams into a new
  Figma
  organization. During this time, you won't be able to access your
  existing
  teams. This process usually takes a couple of minutes, but can take
  longer
  for teams with hundreds of members or lots of resources. We recommend
  you set aside an hour for this task and choose a time which will
  cause
  minimal disruption to your team(s).
- Once your Organization plan subscription is active, you'll be able
  to
  [create new teams](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040450293)
  or upgrade other teams.

**Upgrade to the Organization plan**

1. Open the [file browser](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/14381406380183-Guide-to-the-file-browser) by
   logging in to your account at [figma.com](https://figma.com/).
   - If you’re in a specific Figma file, you can access the file
     browser
     by clicking **Main menu** >
     **Back to files** from the left sidebar toolbar.
2. Click **Admin**.
3. Select the **Billing** tab.
4. Find where your current plan is listed in the top right corner of
   the
   page, and click **upgrade**.
5. In the modal, click **Upgrade and choose seats** for
   the
   Organization plan option.
6. Select which teams you want to include in your new Figma organization.
7. Next, select a seat type for each existing team member. You can also
   select **Add seats to assign later** to add additional,
   unassigned seats to your subscription.
8. Click **Next: Payment**.
9. Enter in your payment information.
10. Click **Next: Review**.
11. Review your plan and subscription details.
12. After agreeing to Figma’s terms of services, click
    **Complete purchase**.

**Upgrade to the Enterprise plan**

The Enterprise plan offers our most advanced security and administration
features. To upgrade to the Enterprise plan, please [get in touch with our sales team](https://www.figma.com/contact/).

## Downgrade or cancel your plan

Note: Downgrading or cancelling your subscription does not automatically delete your account.

[Learn more about account deletion](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039826814).

**Downgrade from the Professional plan**

Cancelling your Professional plan subscription will downgrade your team
to
the free Starter plan. Here are a few things to keep in mind before you
downgrade:

- **File limit**: On the Starter plan, there are the following
  limitations:
  - 3 total Figma Design and Figma Sites files in a project
  - 3 files per each for FigJam and Figma Slides

If you have more than the allowed number of files in your team when you
downgrade,
Figma will prevent you from being able to edit your files. To keep editing
those files, move them to another team or to your team drafts.
[Learn more about moving files](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360038511573).

- **Project limit**: On a Starter plan, your team will
  have
  one project. If your team has more than one project when you downgrade,
  Figma will lock your team and prevent you from editing your files.
  To
  keep editing, you'll need to reorganize your files and projects so
  they
  are under those limits.  
  [Learn more about moving projects](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/12729516837271).
- **Published sites**: Any published sites in
  Figma
  Sites will automatically get unpublished
  when you downgrade.
- **Pubilished apps or prototypes**: Published apps in Figma Make will remain published and available at their figma.site subdomain, but any connected custom domain will be removed.

If you want to continue using Figma on the free Starter team, we recommend
that you do the following:

- Move your files into one project (without exceeding Starter plan
  file
  limits as described above)
- Remove extra projects
- Move files to another team or to your Starter team's drafts
- Download copies of your [account invoices](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040532093)

If you want to delete your team, you’ll need to cancel your Professional
plan subscription first.

To cancel your Professional plan subscription:

1. Using the browser or Figma desktop app, locate the Professional team
   in the file browser sidebar.
2. Click **Admin**.
3. Select the **Settings** tab.
4. In the **Plan** section, click **Cancel plan**.
5. Figma will ask you for a cancellation reason. Select the most appropriate
   reason and click **Cancel plan**.

At the end of your billing period, Figma will downgrade your team to
a free
Starter team. Any seats you add before your plan is downgraded will be
prorated
and reflected on your last invoice. You’ll have access to all Professional
plan features until the end of the current billing period.

**Downgrade from the Organization or Enterprise plan**

To downgrade from the Organization or Enterprise plan,
[contact our support team](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?&chatStartExpanded).

### Refund policy

Payments for Figma subscriptions are non-refundable.

With that said, if for any reason you’re unhappy with Figma, please let us know. Contact us via the  help widget inside of Figma, or [submit a request through our contact form](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?&chatStartExpanded). Give us a detailed account of what happened and we’ll work with you to make sure you have the best experience possible.
