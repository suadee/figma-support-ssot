## 내용

In this video, watch Rachel—a product designer at Cheddar, a budgeting app—work with the Figma agent to transform a landing page wireframe into an immersive, brand-forward experience: building a custom shader for the hero section, generating on-brand product imagery, and adding motion to a key interaction.

In the following articles, we'll take a closer look at each of these methods of adding expressivity and how to use them in your workflow:

- [Build your own shaders and effects](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/41159710233623): use the Figma agent to generate shader fills and effects, then refine them with built-in controls
- [Create imagery that feels on-brand](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/41159711083543): use image tools and Figma Weave to generate high-quality, brand-aligned imagery without leaving the canvas
- [Bring designs to life with motion](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/41159708615319): use the agent to explore motion directions, then fine-tune with Figma Motion

**Tip:** To learn more about the Figma agent in design files—including a complete list of supported features—check out the [guide to working with the Figma agent in design files](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/37998629035799).
