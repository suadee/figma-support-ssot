## 내용

Community resource licenses vary by resource type and if that resource is free or paid. See a breakdown of our different licenses below:

## Files

### Free files

Free files are published under an [Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) license. This isn't a public domain license. Creators may choose to license a file under additional license types in addition to CC by 4.0.

CC by 4.0 means that free files can be shared and adapted for any purpose, including commercial purposes:

- **Share:** Copy and redistribute the material in any medium or format
- **Adapt:** Transform and build upon the material

Under CC BY 4.0, Community members need to give credit to the original creator.

### Paid files

Paid files are published under the [Community Paid Resource License](https://www.figma.com/community-paid-resource-license). Creators may not offer commercial files under any other terms.

In addition to the rights and restrictions set forth in the Community Paid Resource License, Users that purchase a paid file may:

- Share the file contents with up to five editors
- Incorporate the files contents into unlimited outputs (designs, applications, websites, etc.) Any output must be significantly different than the original files contents, require time, effort, and skill to produce, and not derive its primary value from the file contents itself.

Users may not resell or redistribute the file other than as allowed above.

Note: The free preview content included in paid Figma Design files is licensed under [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) as covered in the Free files section above.

## Slide deck templates

Slide deck templates are published under an [Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) license. This isn't a public domain license. Creators may choose to license a template under additional license types in addition to CC by 4.0.

CC by 4.0 means that templates can be shared and adapted for any purpose, including commercial purposes:

- **Share:** Copy and redistribute the material in any medium or format
- **Adapt:** Transform and build upon the material

Under CC BY 4.0, Community members need to give credit to the original creator.

## Plugins

### Free plugins

By default, free plugins are published under the [Community Free Resource License](https://www.figma.com/community-free-resource-license/). Creators may choose to supplement or replace the Community Free Resource License with their own licensing terms.

Under the Community Free Resource License, the plugin may be ran in files that will be used for any purpose. Users may not resell or redistribute the plugin.

Additional terms may apply if the creator has supplemented or replaced the Community Paid Resource License. If you have questions about what the license allows, reach out to the plugin’s creator.

### Paid plugins

Paid plugins are published under the [Community Paid Resource License](https://www.figma.com/community-paid-resource-license). Creators may choose to supplement the Community Paid Resource License with their own licensing terms. Under the Community Paid Resource License, the plugin may be ran in files that will be used for any purpose. Users may not resell, redistribute, or copy the plugin.

Additional terms may apply if the creator has supplemented the Community Paid Resource License. If you have questions about what the license allows, reach out to the plugin’s creator.

## Report a violation

Outside of published remixes, Figma isn't able to track files to ensure appropriate attribution. If you notice that someone is not meeting these requirements, please contact [Figma Support](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041057214-Get-help-with-Figma#Contact_support).
