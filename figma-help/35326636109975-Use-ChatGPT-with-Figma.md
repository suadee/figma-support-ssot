## 내용

Before you start

Who can use this feature

Available on any Figma plan

Available for FigJam and Figma Slides

This feature is currently not available in the EU

[Learn more about who can access AI features in Figma](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24039793359767).

You can create and open Figma files directly from ChatGPT. This integration helps you turn chats into editable Figma files you can share and edit in FigJam or Figma Slides.

When you use ChatGPT to generate content, you can turn prompts into:

- FigJam diagrams
- Figma Slides presentations

ChatGPT only supports integrations with FigJam and Figma Slides. You currently can't use ChatGPT to create files for Figma's other products.

**Note:** The ChatGPT integration with Figma Slides is currently in beta. During beta, there may be usage limits. Full seats on paid plans come with the largest usage allowance.

## Connect the Figma app to ChatGPT

If this is your first time using Figma in ChatGPT, you may be prompted to connect the Figma app directly during a prompt. You can also connect the app manually at any time.

To connect the Figma app to ChatGPT:

1. Open ChatGPT.
2. Open the left side panel and click your profile or username.
3. Select **Apps & connectors**.
4. Scroll until you find **Figma**, then select **Connect**.
5. Follow the on-screen prompts to allow ChatGPT to access Figma.

Tip: Check out [OpenAI’s help center](https://help.openai.com/en/collections/12923329-connected-apps) for more details on connecting apps.

## Generate Figma files in ChatGPT

ChatGPT users can create Figma files directly in their chats.

1. Open a conversation in ChatGPT.
2. Ask for what you want to create. For example, a prompt could be “Create a FigJam flowchart,” or “Make a Figma Slides presentation about our Q4 roadmap.”
   - We recommend explicitly triggering the Figma app for best results. To do so, start your prompt with “Figma”. Alternatively, click the + plus icon next to your prompt and select the Figma app.
3. ChatGPT will generate an embedded preview of your file.
   - You may be directed to [login or sign up for a Figma account](#h_01K6NNBWSPAQPT3BE7W9E3HVTC). You can do so directly from the embedded ChatGPT preview.

If you haven’t connected Figma to ChatGPT before, you’ll be prompted to [set up the connection](#h_01K6NNBWSPFZAP6MD34BGR453B) during prompting. ChatGPT will ask for permission to connect to Figma.

### How prompting works

ChatGPT uses your prompt to decide which tool to use and what kind of file to generate. ChatGPT may ask brief follow-up questions to clarify your intentions, such as your goal or audience.

Currently, Figma in ChatGPT has limited support for generating Figma Slides content using file uploads like images or PDFs. ChatGPT can use information from uploads as context, but can’t always embed uploaded content in file outputs.

- For Figma Slides presentations, ChatGPT turns your prompt into an objective, an outline, and notes on style. Figma uses these inputs to generate a robust, polished presentation you can use as a jumping off point.
  - Example prompts:
    - Turn this notes document into a sleek, modern-looking Figma Slides deck.
    - Make me a Figma Slides deck for a new business meeting to pitch a software product. Use sales strategies from leading tech companies to inform the flow.
    - Generate a minimalist portfolio Figma Slides deck highlighting my experience, skills, and selected projects.
- For FigJam, diagrams are build from your description of what you’re trying to visualize or organize. ChatGPT creates a FigJam file with shapes, connectors, and text labels to help you start mapping your ideas.
  - Example prompts:
    - Turn this napkin drawing into a diagram in FigJam.
    - Read the attached product requirements document to create a flowchart of the 2FA flow in FigJam.
    - I’m prepping for a systems design interview. Can you give me an example FigJam diagram modeling a service-oriented architecture for an E2E payments application?

ChatGPT is best used to generate a structured starting point you can refine in Figma. To edit visuals, add text, or replace content, open the file in Figma after it’s generated.

## Open and edit the file in Figma

Select **Open in Figma** (mobile) or **Edit in Figma** (desktop) from the chat to continue working in FigJam or Figma Slides.

![Click the button on the ChatGPT diagram embed to open the diagram directly in FigJam.](https://help.figma.com/hc/article_attachments/35391667932951)

- If you’re signed in to a Figma account, you can claim the file and add it to your drafts (and select a team if you belong to more than one).
- If you’re not signed in, you can log in or create a new account to claim the file and add it to your drafts.

Files created from ChatGPT that haven’t been claimed are public and viewable by anyone with the link. Once you log in, sign up, or add the file to your drafts, it becomes private to you.

**Note**: You can open and view Figma files on mobile, but we recommend using desktop for editing.
