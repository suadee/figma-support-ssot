## 내용

Before you Start

Who can use this feature

 

Users who have been approved to sell resources on Community

After you’ve been approved to sell on Community, you must activate your Stripe account before you can publish paid resources. You will receive an email inviting you to activate your Stripe account. A banner will also display on the Figma Community with the same prompt.

Note: If you reside in a country that is [not supported by Stripe](https://stripe.com/global), you will not be able to sell on Community.

## Activate your Stripe account

1. Click the prompt to be redirected to the **Metrics** tab on your Community profile.
2. Click **Set up Stripe** to be redirected to the Stripe website.
3. Follow the instructions on the screen to set up your Stripe account.

Note: If you get interrupted at any point during the activation process, you can navigate back to the **Metrics** tab on your Community Profile and click **Set up Stripe** to continue activating your account.

After your Stripe account has been successfully activated, you are ready to start publishing paid resources to the Figma Community.
