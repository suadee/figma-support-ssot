## 내용

The Organization plan is designed for growing teams and businesses that need advanced administrative, security, and collaboration features.

The Organization plan might be right for you if you:

- Are a business that designs products across your organization
- Need unlimited teams and basic security
- Want to centralize assets, libraries, and workflows across departments

## Products and features

On the Organization plan, you have access to the full Figma product suite:

- [Figma Design](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/categories/360002042553)
- [FigJam](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/categories/360002051633)
- [Figma Slides](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/24170630629911-Explore-Figma-Slides)
- [Figma Draw](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31440394517143-Explore-Figma-Draw)
- [Figma Sites](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31230436657815-Explore-Figma-Sites)
- [Figma Buzz](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31271566667543-Guide-to-Figma-Buzz)
- [Figma Make](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/31304412302231-Explore-Figma-Make)
- [Dev Mode](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/15023124644247-Guide-to-Dev-Mode)

You also get access to advanced features not available on lower-tier plans, such as:

- [Branching and merging](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360063144053-Guide-to-branching)
- [Library analytics](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039238353-View-and-explore-library-analytics)
- [Single sign-on (SSO)](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040532333-Guide-to-SAML-SSO)

To better understand which individual features are available on the Organization plan, you can:

- Review Figma’s [pricing and plans page](https://www.figma.com/pricing/)
- Review the “Who can use this feature” box at the top of each Figma Learn article

### Organization plan billing

Only organization admins can manage billing on the Organization plan.

The Organization plan uses an annual subscription with quarterly invoices. Your subscription cost is based on the number of Full, Dev, and Collab seats. View seats are free.

Note: Billing is separate from permissions. A paid seat gives someone access to Figma products, while permissions determine what they can do in specific files or projects.

To learn more:

- [Manage billing on the Organization plan](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040328293)

### Organization plan permissions

In Figma organizations, you can share resources at multiple levels: organization, team, project, file, and prototype.

[Learn more about sharing and permissions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500007609322-Guide-to-sharing-and-permissions).

The following specifications apply to the Organization plan:

- You can invite [members or guests](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420557314967-Members-versus-guests). Guest refers to anyone in the organization that doesn't have a company email, and can have limited access to the organization.
- [Viewer restrictions](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360040045574-Restrict-copying-and-sharing-on-files): Prevent viewers from copying, sharing, or exporting file content.
- [Password protection](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/5726720100247-Add-password-protection-to-files-and-prototypes): Add a password to restrict access to shared files.
- Organization admins can [disable public link sharing for the organization](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/5726756336791-Manage-public-link-sharing-and-open-sessions). This prevents people outside the organization from accessing files via link sharing.
- You can toggle on the option for organization members to be able to discover file via search in the file browser from [advanced share settings](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/1500007609322). If the option to discover a file via search is disabled, organization members will need the direct file link or be invited to the file or project the file is in to be able to access it.
- Draft files are always private until you share them. You can invite collaborators to your draft files with either `can view` or `can edit` access, depending on your [seat type](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/360039960434).
  - For example, if you have a Collab seat, you can invite others to your FigJam and Figma Slides drafts, but not to Figma Design drafts, since a Collab seat does not include Figma Design access.
  - Drafts are owned by the organization. When a member leaves, the organization can [claim ownership of any draft files](https://help.figma.com/hc/en-us/articles/4420549259799).
